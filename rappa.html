Rappa

"My ninja name is Rappa, which means "to be of a steady heart, destroying evil and unveiling righteousness." It is just like the arrows of the Great Lan, far-reaching and all-prevailing."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Rappa is an AoE-oriented break DPS who relies on consistently breaking many small enemies (or a few Elite-level enemies) to maximise her damage output.

The bulk of her damage relies on her enhanced Basic Attacks, which she gets 3 of every time she uses her Ultimate. She gains energy every time she breaks an Elite-level enemy, which means that she excels in fights where there are a lot of Elite-level enemies to break or if there is a lot of energy to get from kills in general. Without consistent sources of energy, however, she has notable downtime on her Ultimate (and in turn her damage output), which makes her ideal matchups quite situational compared to other break DPSes.

Build Notes

ATK: 3200 ATK is a requirement and not a recommendation, since it maxes out the amount of vulnerability Rappa inflicts on enemies when she breaks enemies. Once you reach 3200 ATK, you do not need to build any further ATK.

BE: Subtract BE by 40% if you are using Kalpagni and 20% if you are using Talia.

SPD: The 159.7+ SPD breakpoint for E0S0 is for being able to do 5 EBAs in 350 AV (3 MoC cycles) assuming 1 Skill downtime between Ultimates, while the 166.7+ SPD breakpoint for E0S1 is for being able to do 3 EBAs in 150 AV (1 MoC cycle). You can aim for lower SPD breakpoints with E0S1 Rappa (or use DDD), but these SPD breakpoints provide the best frontloaded damage for both LCs.

Read this spreadsheet for more information about SPD breakpoints with Rappa: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1kg_3PR9vYLVx0NIfqOSjTv2eFBOlBaw3-CUhgSNnE0E/edit?gid=0#gid=0

Rope: There are some cases where an ERR rope can reduce the downtime between Ultimate uses to 1 turn each (or 0 turns most ideally), like being able to break elite-level enemies 6 times in total to reduce Ultimate downtime from 1 turn to 0. This is, however, very fight-dependent, and it comes with each Rappa EBA doing less damage overall though you can theoretically get less downtime in doing so.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Ninjitsu Inscription: Dazzling Evilbreaker S1 BiS
Rappa's signature LC and by far her best option (by at least 20% in sims over the best 4* option, often much higher in shorter fights). The starting energy gives her the ability to use her Ultimate after her first skill (and her Ultimate/enhanced state is needed to accumulate energy faster), and the 50% advance every 2 EBAs allows her to do 3 EBAs in 150 AV at 166.67+ SPD, and it has a high enough base ATK where you only need 1 ATK% stat to reach 3200 ATK.
After the Charmony Fall S5 Alternative
Her best 4* option in terms of consistent damage output in longer fights. Provides a good amount of BE and you get a 16% SPD for 2 turns (which aligns well with her Ultimate since the SPD boost benefits both the 1 extra turn from her Ultimate + 2 natural ones which correspond to her last 2 EBAs), but it requires you to use 2 Skills at the start of the fight to get her Ultimate up, which makes this LC poor for frontloading damage.
Make the World Clamor S5 Alternative
Provides her with enough energy at the start of combat to give her a turn 1 Ultimate, but since it has no other effects that benefit Rappa, this LC is only best for frontloading damage with Rappa.

Relic Sets

Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge BiS
General BiS relic set for Rappa, providing her with BE% and DEF shred for her Break DMG (her main source of damage) and Super Break DMG provided by Fugue, Dahlia or HMC.
Thief of Shooting Meteor Alternative
Situational BiS relic set for Rappa. Has weaker damage amplification than Iron Cavalry overall, but against many enemies like in a Pure Fiction scenario, the energy regeneration (3 from ecah break, including exo-toughness break) from this set can be enough to reduce Ultimate downtime to zero.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD or BE Alternative
For if you don't have any good 4pcs of the above sets.

Planar Ornaments

Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern BiS
BiS planar if you are using The Dahlia with Rappa, which you should be if you plan on using any break team. Provides the highest BE% boost out of any planar while also providing 6% SPD, which is integral for certain SPD breakpoints with Rappa.
Talia: Kingdom of Banditry Alternative
Her second best planar, though it is her BiS planar if you are not using Dahlia. Provides up to 36% BE if you hit 145 SPD, which you should be if you are using Rappa.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
For frontloading 2 EBAs in 150 AV without her S1 or Make the World Clamor. Generally not recommended as the BE loss is often too drastic.

Her best team with Fugue providing exo-toughness and a bit of Super Break damage, while The Dahlia provides Weakness Break Efficiency (necessary for Rappa to cut down toughness) and a lot of Super Break damage overall. Lingsha is used as a general sustain which provides decent toughness damage, but DHPT can be used if you need Physical toughness damage instead. The Dahlia and Fugue are essential supports for Rappa, while the 4th unit can either be Lingsha (for sustain and potentially some toughness damage) or HMC for more Super Break damage and potential DDD procs. Ruan Mei can also be used, but Ruan Mei's extended breaks have anti-synergy with Rappa wanting to break enemies multiple times if she cannot kill those enemies by her exo-toughness break.
