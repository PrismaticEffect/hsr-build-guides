Character Summary

Black Swan is a DoT-based unit that deals damage primarily based on her Arcana debuff, which is accumulated through enemies taking DoT damage through detonations or on their turns.

With her Novaflare, Black Swan can now be played SP-positively and her debuffs are applied on enemy summon or any attack that she uses, but she is still heavily reliant on detonators like Kafka and frequent DoT applicators like Hysilens for maximum damage.

Build Notes

Prisoner 4pc and Revelry 2pc are assumed for these stats. If you are using Pan-Cosmic instead, subtract the ATK recommendations by 280 at S0 and 310 at S1.

120% EHR for Black Swan is basically a requirement due to her A2 trace, not a suggestion. Beyond 120% EHR, focus more on ATK or SPD.

SPD: 160+ SPD Swan is only recommended for higher relic investment, or you can focus more on EHR and using an ATK body instead. A higher SPD Swan is more useful in modes like Pure Fiction or Apocalyptic Shadow, since enemies spawn there more frequently and Swan might need to attack more often to generate SP, add Arcana faster or to apply debuffs during phase changes sooner.

If you are using Hysilens S1 on either Swan or Hysilens, you can aim for 128.5 SPD or 152.5 SPD instead for an effective 133.34/160 SPD Swan in wave 1 of MoC.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Reforged Remembrance S1 BiS
Her signature LC and her best unconditional LC at E0 level. Provides ATK and DEF shred, though she overcaps on DEF shred with this LC in her premium team (112.6% DEF shred).
Those Many Springs S1 Alternative
Jiaoqiu's signature LC and second best LC at E0 level. Provides a massive 60% EHR boost which makes it more feasible to use an ATK body instead of an EHR body to improve Swan's offensive stats. This LC also provides a 24% vulnerability debuff, but it requires Swan to attack enemies to inflict the debuff, which can be difficult to do against bosses with summons or phase changes.
Why Does The Ocean Sing S1 Alternative
Situationally best LC at E1 level if you have Reforged Remembrance and E1 on Hysilens as well. At E1, she is able to accumulate 6 debuffs, which maximises the vulnerability effect of this LC, and both Hysilens and Black Swan do not overcap on DEF shred if their signature LCs were swapped. This reduces Swan's damage overall but increases Hysilens' damage, which can result in a net damage increase if enemies are Physical weak or are summoned frequently like in Pure Fiction (since Hysilens' DoT damage have less rampup time than Swan).
Eyes of the Prey S5 Alternative
General best 4* option as it provides EHR and DMG (both stats that Swan really wants), about a 15% damage loss from her signature LC.
Good Night and Sleep Well S5 Alternative
Provides 72% DMG for Swan and theoretically can be better than Eyes of the Prey S5 if you manage to hit Swan's EHR requirement without an EHR-boosting cone, but it requires far too many substats for most builds to accomplish without a notable damage loss.

Relic Sets

Prisoner in Deep Confinement BiS
Dedicated DoT set and overall BiS relic set for Swan. Set provides ATK and DEF shred which Swan wants.
ATK 2pc / DMG 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have a Prisoner 4pc, but it is highly recommended to farm Prisoner if you plan on running a DoT team in general.

Planar Ornaments

Revelry by the Sea BiS
Strongest unconditional DoT planar, providing 12% ATK and 24% DoT DMG.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
A good alternative if you do not have Revelry pieces since it provides 12% ATK and some DMG, but it requires Swan to be built with at least 135 SPD (for +12% DMG), and 160 SPD for the maximum DMG boost (18%) from this planar. In triple DoT, Revelry by the Sea is a 2% improvement over Glamoth (135 SPD) and Pan-Cosmic. The difference is less than 1.5% for Glamoth (160 SPD).
Pan-Cosmic Commercial Enterprise Alternative
A good alternative if you do not have Revelry pieces, providing 10% EHR and 25% ATK to Swan. But due to Kafka giving 100% ATK to Swan, this planar ends up performing slightly worse than Revelry in DoT teams.

Sustain
With 4.0 buffs, Black Swan becomes the strongest 3rd unit for DoT in terms of raw damage output for teams with sustain. However, raw damage output can still be affected by wave/phase changes and summons, but not large enough to make Swan a considerably worse option than supports such as Cipher, Tribbie or Cyrene. DHPT helps with providing ATK to an ally (usually Swan or Hysilens) while providing enough attacks to trigger Kafka's detonations through her FUAs. Kafka and Hysilens are currently essential for DoT teams, while DHPT can be replaced with Huohuo if unloading Kafka's FUAs is not a problem or if you need more energy sources. Other sustain alternatives include Hyacine or Gallagher for their high attack frequency and high SPD.
