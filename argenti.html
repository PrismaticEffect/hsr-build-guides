Character Summary

Argenti is a AoE-based Ultimate DPS who has two different Ultimates; a weaker AoE ultimate which costs less energy, and a stronger Ultimate which has a stronger AoE attack and additional bounce damage which helps for lower target fights. He also gains Energy based on the number of targets he hits with his attacks, which means that he particularly excels in AoE scenarios.

As of early 4.x, Argenti is primarily relegated to gamemodes like Pure Fiction, where it is a 5-target fight (his best case scenario) with multiple weaker enemies at a time, though he primarily uses his Skill and weaker Ultimate in those fights to take out weaker enemies (which in turn contributes to taking out the boss of each wave). In lower-target fights, his weaker Ultimate does not do enough damage, and he cannot generate enough energy to get to his stronger Ultimate quickly and consistently enough even if he takes multiple turns in a rotation with Sunday.

Build Notes

The recommended build here is for hypercarry Argenti in a Pure Fiction-only scenario, which assumes Eagle 4pc with ATK boots/ERR rope, which is his current best DPS build. With Sunday, you use Scholar 4pc with SPD boots/ATK rope instead. For a build where Argenti's main purpose is to battery The Herta, run 4pc Eagle with ERR rope, but aim for 160 SPD instead (and ignore all offensive stat recommendations).

CR/CD: If you are using Sunday, aim for 55% CR out of combat instead and add 30% CD to your recommended CD stat (based on Argenti's investment). If you are using RMC, modify the recommended CR/CD by -10%/+20%.

SPD: With 4pc Eagle, you do not need to build SPD on Argenti, though extra SPD is nice to have. If you are using him with Sunday, do not use Eagle and run a 133.34+ SPD Argenti with a -1 SPD Sunday setup instead.

Rope: With 4pc Eagle, you generally prefer to run ERR rope on him to spam as many Ultimates as possible. ERR rope provides him with 78 energy with 1 ult + skill, which means he needs 12 more energy from other sources (3 PF kills) to get his level 1 Ultimate back. If you are using Sunday, use an ATK rope instead.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Into the Unreachable Veil S1 BiS
The Herta's LC and general BiS LC for DPS Argenti (20% better than Repose), as it provides 12% CR and 60% DMG to both his Skill and Ultimate, both being notable damage sources for him. In Pure Fiction, you are only expected to use his 90-energy ult, so the SP refund part is useless, but it can have some value in non-AoE fights.
An Instant Before A Gaze S1 Alternative
Second best LC for Argenti, since it provides 36% CD and 64% Ultimate DMG, but given that his Skill is 1/3 of his damage profile in PF, this does worse than The Herta's LC for him.
Today is Another Peaceful Day S5 Alternative
Good BP LC option since it provides 64% DMG to both Argenti's Skill and Ultimate.
A Dream Scented in Wheat S5 Alternative
Alternative BP LC option for Argenti since it provides 20% CR and 40% Ultimate DMG, but this can theoretically be worse than Another Peaceful Day S5 with a well-built Argenti.
Geniuses' Repose S5 Alternative
Best non-BP 4* LC since it provides the best offensive buffs out of all of the 4* options. Full uptime on the LC's secondary effect is assumed.

Relic Sets

Eagle of Twilight Line BiS
Best overall set for Argenti in Pure Fiction and similar (5-target) scenarios. Ideally, you should be able to get a 90-energy Ultimate 1 time or more per Skill (higher with kill energy), which leads to at least an effective 33% SPD increase while being on ATK boots, often higher in practice.
Scholar Lost in Erudition Alternative
Good set for Argenti and provides him with both CR and DMG to both his Skill and Ultimate. This set is better used when paired with Sunday, since speed tuning Sunday around Argenti + Eagle is inconsistent and some advance from Eagle can be wasted.

Planar Ornaments

Sigonia, the Unclaimed Desolation BiS
Best planar in Pure Fiction scenarios. Provides 4% CR and up to 40% CD to Argenti.
Inert Salsotto Alternative
Generalist planar for Argenti since it doesn't rely on enemy kills; provides 8% CR and 15% Ultimate DMG to Argenti. Slightly worse than Sigonia (<2%) assuming max secondary buffs on Sigonia, but Salsotto can be used in more than just Pure Fiction.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
Only if you hit 135 SPD (and thus, paired with Sunday). Provides a 12% ATK boost and 12% DMG.

Investment Recommendation

E0S1 Argenti is the highest vertical investment you should go on Argenti in particular. For further investment, aim for E1 Tribbie, and E1 Robin for sustainless teams (or you could use Robin in lieu of RMC in Pure Fiction teams as well at this investment).
