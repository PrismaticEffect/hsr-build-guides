Character Summary

Blade is a HP scaling DPS who specialises in blast/AoE scenarios. Blade loses HP through various means (his Skill, his Enhanced Basic, and sometimes his Ultimate), which all contribute to his Talent. His Talent keeps track of the number of times he has lost HP, and after reaching a certain number of times, he triggers his AoE FUA, which makes him a decently all-rounded unit.

However, due to his Talent being contingent on frequent HP loss and is a notable source of his damage, supportive units that drain his HP such as Hyacine E0S1 or RMC with Hyacine's LC greatly improve his performance as he can trigger his Talent far more often.

Build Notes

HP: Recommended stats here assume HP boots. Aim for 6400 HP with SPD boots/DMG orb at E0S0 and 6700 HP at E0S1.

CR/CD: Subtract 16% CR if you are using Longevous Disciple and 28% CD if you are using the Bone Collection planar.

Boots: Always use HP boots unless you are using a -1 SPD Sunday. If you are using a -1 SPD Sunday, aim for 133.34 SPD instead, and aim for 80% CR/160% CD out of combat at E0S0 and 80% CR/190% CD at E0S1.

Orb: DMG% is better than HP% by about 3-4%, but the difference is smaller with Sunday + SPD boots.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

The Unreachable Side S1 BiS
His signature LC and provides him with all of the stats that he wants (HP%, CR%, DMG%). Balancing CR/CD can be a bit difficult with this LC, though.
A Secret Vow S5 Alternative
Generally better than the most F2P option at 50% uptime, but performance is a bit inconsistent since it depends on how often you can trigger the secondary effect of this LC (additional DMG if Blade's HP percentage is lower than the enemy's). Performs better in Pure Fiction scenarios or with better gameplay.
Ninja Record: Sound Hunt S5 Alternative
F2P option (10% worse than his signature LC) and most accessible LC choice for HP scaling Destruction units. Provides some HP% and CD%, both of which Blade benefits from.
