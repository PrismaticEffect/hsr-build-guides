Character Summary

Kafka is an enabler of the DoT (damage over time) archetype as she has the ability to trigger/detonator existing DoTs inflicted onto enemies outside of their turns through multiple means (Skill, Talent, Ultimate). While she herself provides a DoT of her own, her personal DoT damage falls very short compared to more DPS-oriented DoT characters such as Hysilens and Black Swan.

At the moment, Kafka is the only character with easy access to strong DoT detonations, which makes her the cornerstone of the DoT archetype. You cannot run DoT without her, but at the same time, Kafka herself requires DoT DPSes such as Hysilens and/or Black Swan to enable DoT as a playstyle.

Build Notes

This build is for an ERR rope Kafka (her current meta build), which focuses more on her being a DoT detonator than a DPS. Prioritise hitting 75% EHR above all else, as it provides 100% ATK to Kafka.

ATK: ATK is more of a luxury for her more than anything since she only contributes to 4-5% of the team's overall damage. You can forgo an ATK body or offensive orb and use a tank (HP/DEF) body or orb to make Kafka more tanky instead.

SPD: Speed recommendations are for specific SPD breakpoints with Eagle. Most notably, you can get 3 actions in 150 AV and 2 Eagle procs with 166.67 SPD, and you can get 5 actions in 250 AV with 4 Eagle procs and 160 SPD. If you are using Hysilens' LC on Swan or Hysilens, you can reduce the SPD requirements by 10. If you are using 2pc/2pc SPD combos, aim for 160 SPD or 171.43 SPD.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Before the Tutorial Mission Starts S5 BiS
F2P cone and her unconditional best LC option. With Hysilens (who provides DEF shred on enemy spawn), this LC enables her to get 1-turn Ultimates assuming that she gets 10 energy from other sources, which maximises the effectiveness of Eagle 4pc and the team's overall damage output.
Lies Dance on the Breeze S1 Alternative
Cipher's signature LC. Theoretically a good option if there is no way of getting 1-turn Ultimates with Tutorial, but you are almost always getting 1-turn Ultimates with Kafka as long as enemies attack or you get kill energy, which makes this a generally inferior option to Tutorial.
Patience Is All You Need S1 Alternative
Kafka's signature LC, only becomes better than Tutorial at E4 Kafka. Otherwise, this LC fails to give Kafka 1-turn Ultimates, which results in a net damage loss.

Relic Sets

Eagle of Twilight Line BiS
The highest effective SPD increase on Kafka even assuming solely 2-turn Ultimate rotations on Kafka. This set provides a 14.3% total (not just base) SPD increase with just 2-turn ultimates, and a 33.3% total SPD increase with 1-turn Ultimates.
SPD 2pc / SPD 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have a strong Eagle set.

Planar Ornaments

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas BiS
Provides 5% ERR (integral for 1-turn Ultimate rotations) and provides a small amount of ATK to one of your DoT DPSes (Hysilens or Swan).
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Provides 5% ERR and the 40% advance at the start of Wave 1 can be useful for Wave 1 if you don't hit 166.67 SPD or for AA/PF scenarios in general.
Penacony, the Land of Dreams Alternative
Provides 5% ERR, but worst of the ERR planar options because none of the existing DoT DPSes are Lightning.
