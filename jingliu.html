Jingliu

"Whoever wishes to learn my swordplay, I will teach them."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Jingliu is a HP scaling DPS who specialises in blast scenarios. After a certain number of attacks, Jingliu enters her enhanced state, where all of her attacks drain other allies' HP, and ally HP loss frequency is a key driver in maintaining her enhanced state.

Due to Jingliu's Talent being contingent on frequent HP loss and her enhanced state being mandatory for Jingliu to deal damage, supportive units that drain ally HP such as Hyacine E0S1 or RMC with Hyacine's LC greatly improve her overall performance.

Build Notes

HP: Recommended stats here assume 6 HP substats, HP body, HP boots, DMG orb and HP rope. You can use a CD body instead for a slight damage (2-3%) loss, but it is not recommended if you are using Mydei's LC on Jingliu (unless you hit 8,334+ HP).

CR/CD: Subtract 28% CD from recommended CD if you are using the Bone Collection planar.

Boots: Always use HP boots unless you are using a -1 SPD Sunday. If you are using a -1 SPD Sunday, aim for 133.34 SPD instead, and aim for 30% CR/190% CD (or more HP) out of combat.

Orb: DMG% is better than HP% by about 4-5%, but the difference is smaller with Sunday + SPD boots.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Flame of Blood, Blaze My Path S1 BiS
Mydei's signature LC and generally Jingliu's best LC post-Novaflare. Provides HP% and DMG (both stats that Jingliu notably wants), and the HP self-drain from this LC also helps with generating Syzygy stacks to extend her enhanced state or reaching 4 stacks of Syzygy for increased DEF shred.
I Shall Be My Own Sword S1 Alternative
Jingliu's signature LC, which provides CD and DEF shred. Provides a decent amount of DMG% (which Jingliu wants), a bit of DEF shred and a bit of CD. About 6% worse than Mydei's LC without additional DEF shred sources, but the difference is smaller with more DEF shred sources like Silver Wolf or Cipher.
Ninja Record: Sound Hunt S5 Alternative
F2P option (22% worse than Mydei's LC) and most accessible LC choice for HP scaling Destruction units.

Relic Sets

Scholar Lost in Erudition BiS
General best set for Jingliu as it provides DMG% to both her Skill and Ultimate. The CR% helps with building her slightly with a HP/CD body, but it can be difficult to build around if you are using Sunday or RMC with her.
Hunter of Glacial Forest Alternative
Good set for Jingliu as it provides a bit of DMG% and CD (though the CD boost does not have full uptime). About 2.5-3% worse than Scholar 4pc.
Crit 2pc / Crit 2pc or HP 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs, would still be a slight damage downgrade (4%).
Genius of Brilliant Stars Alternative
Only better than Scholar Lost in Erudition with notable DEF shred sources like Silver Wolf + a Quantum-weak enemy, otherwise comparable to Crit 2pc / HP 2pc combos.

Planar Ornaments

Bone Collection's Serene Demesne BiS
Strongest buffing planar for Jingliu since she easily meets the secondary requirement (for 28% CD) and gains a decent amount of HP from this planar.
Rutilant Arena Alternative
About 2% worse than Bone Collection in theory, but building around the CR boost of this planar is a bit tricky with her current 4pc or if you are using CR-providing supports like Sunday or RMC.
