Archer

"First meeting, huh? Just call me Archer. Lucky for us, we're not here to kill each other, so let's keep things civil, if we can."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Archer is a DPS that relies on consuming a large number of skill points in a turn to maximise his attack frequency and, in turn, damage output. As a result, SP economy/management is a big part of his gameplay, and you can lose out on a lot of damage if you do not generate enough skill points on time through supports like Sparkle or SP-positive units.

Since Archer does multiple attacks in a turn, he is usable in many types of enemy scenarios (from 1 target to AoE), though good gameplay is required to ensure efficient damage allocation between Archer's attacks. Regardless, he best excels at low target scenarios.

Build Notes

Archer gains 120% CD in his kit, and his teammates (Sparkle or RMC) all provide various amounts of CD. As a result, CR substats are generally more valuable than CD substats until you reach 100% CR.

Regarding supports, Sparkle's BiS LC for an Archer team is Sunday's LC (A Grounded Ascent) and not her signature LC (Earthly Escapade).

Orb: DMG orb is about 3-4% better than ATK orb, but the difference is smaller with E1 Archer since Archer can use more Skills with a 200% DMG buff. Use the orb with better substats.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

The Hell Where Ideals Burn S1 BiS
Signature LC and general unconditional BiS for him, providing him with CR and ATK (both stats that he generally lacks).
The Finale of a Lie S1 Alternative
Ashveil's signature LC and Archer's theoretical BiS LC at E1. Provides a higher CR boost than his signature, but maintaining full uptime on the secondary effect on this cone requires Archer to get his ultimate every 2 turns, which is only more feasible with E1 Archer or Sunday LC on Sparkle. If you can maintain full uptime on the secondary effect, this is 5-10% stronger than Archer's signature depending on existing vulnerability sources.
See You at the End S5 Alternative
BP option that gives CD and DMG to most of Archer's kit (minus his Ultimate). S4+ of this LC is better than Cruising assuming 75% uptime on the ATK boost.
Cruising in the Stellar Sea S5 Alternative
Best F2P option. Archer can often maintain 75%+ uptime on the secondary ATK boost of this LC assuming that he kills an enemy every 3 turns. You can build around the secondary 16% CR buff of this LC, but it makes runs much less consistent and is not recommended.

Relic Sets

Genius of Brilliant Stars BiS
General best set for Archer and synergises well with supports that provide DEF shred such as Silver Wolf or Cipher.
Scholar Lost in Erudition Alternative
Good and efficient set to farm. Comparable performance to Genius if Archer is against non-Quantum weak enemies and with no DEF shred sources, but otherwise a ~5% damage loss from Genius.
Wavestrider Captain Alternative
Good and efficient set to farm, though this is consistently strong only with E1 Archer or Sunday's LC on Sparkle. If Archer is able to use his Ultimate every 2 turns, this becomes stronger than Scholar but still weaker than Genius if enemies have Quantum weakness.
Poet of Mourning Collapse Alternative
Archer doesn't go below 95 SPD with this set, but Archer gains 20% CR and 10% DMG from this regardless. However, speed tuning with Sparkle with DDD or Eagle might be more difficult as Archer may not take an immediate turn.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.

Planar Ornaments

Tengoku@Livestream BiS
Provides the best unconditional offensive buffs out of all of the options available for Archer.
Rutilant Arena Alternative
Second best unconditional option, providing 8% CR and a minor Skill DMG boost for Archer. 1.5-2% worse than Tengoku@Livestream.
The Wondrous BananAmusement Park Alternative
An option that gives the same buffs as Tengoku@Livestream, but it locks you into using DHPT.
