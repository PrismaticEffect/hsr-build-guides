Cipher

"Cifera? Ah, so you've heard of me... But few call me by that name these days. Call me Cipher. It's sharper and swifter, like the wind itself. A perfect name for a master thief, don't you think?"
★★★★★

Character Summary

Cipher is a very SP-positive support that provides vulnerability and records a proportion of damage inflicted in the fight (as a damage tally). With her Ultimate, she inflicts damage to enemies equal to the amount of damage that she has recorded since the last Ultimate (or the start of the fight if she has not used her Ultimate at all).

While Cipher herself does not do much personal damage at E0S0/E0S1 level, her high SPD, general SP positivity, good toughness damage and widely applicable debuffs makes her usable in various teams, though it might not always be the best option in every team. Her damage tally is also useful for efficiently taking out certain bosses if planned properly.

Build Notes

Recommended stats assume 2pc SPD/2pc SPD for E0S0 and 4pc Pioneer for E0S1.

ATK/CR/CD: Subtract 4% CR/32% CD from E0S1 recommendations if you are using 4pc Pioneer. You can forgo all offensive stats and aim for tankiness and SPD instead if you do not care about Cipher's personal damage.

SPD: 170 SPD is a must to ensure that Cipher gains 50% CR in combat and increasing her damage tally record rate by 100%. You can aim for 200 SPD and forgoing all offensive stats, though this is only recommended with 2pc/2pc SPD combos and S1.

EHR: EHR is generally not a requirement because her debuffs are multi-hit and she has multiple opportunities to land her debuffs (either Pearls or S1), unless you are using Holiday Thermae Escape in an Acheron team. If you are using HTE in an Acheron team, aim for 60%+ EHR (with an EHR body).

Rope: ERR rope can be used if you plan on using Cipher's Ultimate more than once, but it is very fight-dependent. ATK rope is for damage, HP/DEF rope is for tankiness.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Lies Dance on the Breeze S1 BiS
Her signature LC and general best LC. Provides her with a notable amount of SPD (18% SPD) which is what she really wants and 24% DEF shred with god uptime. Mandatory LC for Acheron teams as it is the only non-BP LC that provides consistent debuffs.
Resolution Shines as Pearls of Sweat S5 Alternative
Her best 4* LC for damage amp, but the debuff cannot be reapplied when it is already present on an enemy, which makes debuff generation for Acheron inconsistent. Provides 16% DEF shred to enemies, though uptime is a bit inconsistent if the debuff is applied on enemy turns.
Holiday Thermae Escapade S3-S5 Alternative
Provides vulnerability to all enemies. Only slightly stronger damage amp than Pearls S5 with no DEF shred sources, but it requires Cipher to wear an EHR body to consistently land the debuff. For Acheron teams, this is a suitable replacement for her signature LC as this LC can provide debuffs on any attack.

Relic Sets

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters BiS
Provides best personal damage for Cipher.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
Provides 12% SPD total and makes it easier for E0S0 Cipher to hit 170 SPD without compromising on damage. Alternatively, this set is generally best for hitting 200 SPD with Cipher S1, though you might compromise on Cipher's damage in doing so.

Planar Ornaments

City of Converging Stars BiS
Provides the best teamwide buffs assuming that you kill an enemy within 75-100AV.
Lushaka, the Sunken Seas Alternative
Provides 12% ATK to an ally. The ERR is generally not all that useful since you're only using her Ultimate once, but it can be useful if you are using her Ultimate multiple times for specific rotations.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
For if you want her to act first or act 3 times in Wave 1 without hitting 200 SPD.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
Best for personal damage.

Investment Recommendation

E0S1 is generally her recommended stopping point, but you can stop at E1S1 if you can optimise her damage tally, and E2S1 makes her a very competent support for all teams. Regardless, there are usually better investment for DPSes or other supports past E0S1 Cipher.
