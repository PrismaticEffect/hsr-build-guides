Character Summary

Dan Heng Permansor Terrae (or DHPT for short) is an ATK-scaling shielder. In his base kit, he assigns an ally as a Bondmate and provides an ATK buff proportionate to his ATK. DHPT also gives the Bondmate a summon named Souldragon, which can occasionally deal damage based on the Bondmate's stats. Souldragon also provides shields (DHPT's passive way of providing shields) and cleanses negative statuses from allies.

Since DHPT provides a summon, he synergises well with Sunday, who gives an additional 50% DMG to units with summons, and enables DPSes to wear the BananAmusement Park planar, which is often comparably good to BiS choices for many DPSes, if not better than other alternatives.

Build Notes

ATK: Specific ATK recommendations for other LCs will be provided in the recommended LCs section.

SPD: 160+ SPD Is more recommended for teams that want better SP generation (Archer) or high attack frequency (DoT). If you do not need the SP or attack frequency (e.g. Phainon teams), you can go for 142.86 SPD instead, or 133.34 SPD with ATK boots.

Rope: Use ATK rope if you are using him as a sustain for Phainon teams, though you should ideally not be using a sustain with Phainon in the first place.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Though Worlds Apart S1 BiS
His signature LC and his unconditional best LC. Provides a notable ATK boost (the only Preservation LC to do this), a small DMG% buff to all allies (a higher one for his Bondmate), and emergency healing on Ultimate use.
Journey Forever Peaceful S3-S5 Alternative
BP LC which provides shield strength and an unconditional DMG% buff to all allies. It is most recommended to use this cone only at S3 or higher, which is where shielding is comparable to 635 base ATK cones. At S1-S2 level, shielding is only comparable to 582 ATK base cones if not worse. If you do not care about shield strength, however, this is DHPT's strongest offensive option other than his S1. Aim for 2800 ATK with this cone.
635 Base ATK LC Alternative
Any LC with 635 or more base ATK (of any path), which is reserved for certain premium DPS lightcones. Aim for 3450+ ATK.
582 Base ATK LC Alternative
Any LC with 582 or more base ATK (of any path), which can be found through standard 5* lightcones such as Welt's or Yanqing's.
Moment of Victory S1 Alternative
Niche option for DoT teams since this LC provides EHR, which synergises well with Kafka's 100% ATK boost to DHPT if you hit 75% EHR. This makes this LC stronger than a 635 base ATK LC for DoT only. Outside of DoT, this LC is worse than a 582 base ATK LC. If you use this build, use an EHR body and aim for 75% EHR, 160 SPD and 2450+ ATK out of combat.

Relic Sets

Self-Enshrouded Recluse BiS
His best set for shielding as it provides a 22% increase to shield strength while also providing a 15% CD boost to all allies. General best set for most teams.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
His best set for general SP generation as it provides the highest SPD increase out of all relic combinations at E0S0. Best suited for Archer and DoT teams.
Sacerdos' Relived Ordeal Alternative
His Skill counts as a buff to a single target, so if you have the SP to use, DHPT can theoretically wear this set to provide your hypercarry DPS a bit of CD. This is best used if your DPS is paired with Sparkle/RMC rather than Sunday (or no advancers at all), or is able to freeze buff duration (like Phainon).
Warrior Goddess of Sun and Thunder Alternative
His best set for SP generation at S1 level only. Since his LC heals, he can activate the +6% SPD effect and provide 15% CD to all allies, making it on par with Recluse 4pc, but with more SPD instead of stronger shielding. Best suited for Archer teams.

Planar Ornaments

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas BiS
Most all-rounded planar for DHPT. Gives a bit of ERR% to DHPT and provides an ally with 12% ATK.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
The best offensive planar for DHPT if you don't need ERR from a planar. Gives a bit of ATK to DHPT and provides the team with a bit of CD when there is an enemy.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Provides a bit of ERR% to DHPT though the action advance is not very useful unless you have a low SPD DHPT yet you want DHPT to act 3 times in wave 1. Most recommended if you want DHPT to act first in the team (like in DoT teams).
Penacony, the Land of Dreams Alternative
Provides a bit of ERR% and a bit of DMG% for Physical DPSes like Phainon. Good planar for Physical-based DPSes.

Investment Recommendation

E0S0 is already decently strong as a sustain. If you want to make DHPT a better sustain, aim for E0S1 (or E2S0 if you have the funds). E1S0 is also good and quite strong for Archer teams. Outside of that, you are usually better off investing into your DPSes or supports more. Refer to specific characters' investment recommendations for more information.
