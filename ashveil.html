Character Summary

Ashveil is an SP-neutral unit that provides damage amplification (Crit DMG and DEF shred) while dealing decent damage on his own. While his kit is geared towards being a supportive or sub-DPS unit, he is currently best played as a main DPS unit as there are no strong DPSes that complement his overall playstyle.

Most of his damage is gated through his Ultimate, which gives him 3 strong FUAs and an enhanced FUA. However, he gains energy through allies (including himself) attacking an enemy that he marks with Bait (through his Talent or his Skill), which means that he is best complemented with frequent attackers or units that provide energy for him.

Build Notes

Recommended stats are out of combat, but if you use advancers that increase CR such as Sunday or RMC, reduce the CR recommendation accordingly (-10% for RMC, -20% for Sunday). Ashveil gains up to 120% CD in combat, so focus more on CR over CD. He needs 16 CR substats and a CR body with Cruising S5 to hit 100% CR without further CR sources. The ATK recommendation is for 1 ATK main stat and 2 ATK main stats respectively.

Boots: Use ATK boots if you are using Sparkle or RMC with Ashveil. SPD boots is only recommended with a -1 SPD setup. For a sub-DPS Ashveil team, Ashveil prefers SPD boots.

Orb: DMG orb is best with Sparkle or RMC as your sole advancer, but ATK is marginally better with Sunday/DHPT.

Rope: ATK rope is best, but ERR rope can be preferred if you are able to get one extra ultimate through an ERR rope within a certain timeframe. However, the utility of an ERR rope is diminished since his energy gain from ally attacks is not affected by ERR.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

The Finale of a Lie S1 BiS
Best generalist LC (25% improvement over Cruising with no secondary buffs) for both main DPS and sub-DPS Ashveil, providing most of the stats that Ashveil wants (ATK and CR) while having good supportive damage amplification.
Baptism of Pure Thought S1 Alternative
Theoretical best BiS LC for main DPS Ashveil due to its overall damage amp (DEF shred and good DMG%), but Ashveil requires 22 CR substats to reach 100% CR with this LC without Sunday or RMC. If you are lacking CR, you are better off using a different cone.
Worrisome, Blissful S1 Alternative
10% improvement from Cruising with no secondary effects, but performance can be slightly lower if you're fighting multiple targets since the debuff from this LC is single target and requires ramp-up time.
Cruising in the Stellar Sea S5 Alternative
Best F2P option. You can build around the secondary 16% CR buff of this LC, but it makes runs much less consistent and is not recommended.

Relic Sets

The Ashblazing Grand Duke BiS
Unconditional best set for him which provides him with a notable ATK boost (which Ashveil wants) and some DMG%.
Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters Alternative
Second best unconditional option for him (4-5% damage loss from Duke) as he inflicts 3 debuffs in total.
Crit 2pc / Crit 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.
Genius of Brilliant Stars Alternative
Under best conditions (with additional DEF shred sources like Ratio's LC, Cipher E0S1 or Sparkle E2 and Quantum-weak enemies), this can be on par with Duke 4pc, but under poor conditions, this set performs worse than Pioneer.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
Strictly for sub-DPS Ashveil (when paired with Feixiao), and even then, it might not always be the best option if you don't hit any notable SPD breakpoints with this set.

Planar Ornaments

Duran, Dynasty of Running Wolves BiS
BiS planar for main DPS Ashveil with very fast ramp-up time.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
BiS planar for sub-DPS Ashveil. For main DPS Ashveil, this performs comparably to Duran but only if you kill an enemy within 75-100 AV.
The Wondrous BananAmusement Park Alternative
Comparable performance to Duran but locks you to DHPT.
Inert Salsotto Alternative
2% worse than Duran, but a decent option due to its CR and DMG boost to most of Ashveil's damage if you don't have any alternative planars to use.
