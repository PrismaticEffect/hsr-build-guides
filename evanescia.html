Character Summary

Evanescia is an Ultimate-based and primarily Elation DMG character. Her kit revolves around generating Certified Banger stacks for herself (which is one of her sources of Elation DMG damage amplification), which she can obtain when other allies gain Certified Banger through Aha Instants or specific Elation support abilities. Her Certified Banger stacks are then converted into Energy, which can then be used to activate her Ultimate. Gaining Energy also increases her Certified Banger stacks, which further amplifies her damage output.

Evanescia's damage profile is primarily AoE (Elation Skill and Ultimate), though her Ultimate has Bounce damage as well, and more bounces are added to her Ultimate in lower targets, making her a very potent damage dealer especially in 1 target and 5 target fights.

Build Notes

CR/CD: Evanescia gains 30% CR in combat, and in addition to her CR traces, she needs 16 CR substats to reach 70% CR out of combat.

SPD: Since most of Evanescia's damage is out of turn and she does not want to take many turns, SPD is not a high priority for her. However, you can still run a 133.34+ SPD build on her for 2 actions within 150 AV (since ATK doesn't benefit her much), which can squeeze in a bit more damage in some scenarios.

Orb: Difference between DMG and ATK orb is around 1% at best since Elation DMG is not affected by either of these, use the orb with better substats. Alternatively, you can forgo an offensive orb entirely and use a defensive (HP/DEF) orb for better sustain in exchange for a small chunk of her damage.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Until the Flowers Bloom Again S1 BiS
Evanescia's signature LC and her best LC option by some distance. Provides a massive 60% CD, 20.8% ERR (which usually leads to 1 extra Ultimate in 150 AV), and 15% general vulnerability. About 24-30% better than F2P options.
Today's Good Luck S3-S5 Alternative
BP option and while theoretically an offensively better option than other F2P options for her, this LC provides 16-20% at S3-S5, which makes it very easy for Evanescia to overcap on Crit Rate in particular. Generally not recommended unless you have relics that enable her not to overcap on CR with this LC.
Tomorrow, Together S5 Alternative
F2P option for Evanescia and her general best option at low relic investment, provides unconditional 24% CD and 12% Elation to all allies on Ultimate use for 1 turn. Secondary buff uptime on this LC can be a bit inconsistent if not timed properly, and given how fast Evanescia's teammates are, the Elation boost is likely only sticking onto Evanescia herself.
Mushy Shroomy's Adventures S5 Alternative
Alternative F2P option for Evanescia, better for higher relic investment builds. Provides a higher Elation boost (20%) and provides vulnerability for Elation DMG, but it does not provide any CD for Evanescia, which may be more beneficial for lower CD builds.

Relic Sets

Ever-Glorious Magical Girl BiS
Her BiS relic set with very few alternatives (6-7% better than 2pc combos). At least 80% of her damage is Elation DMG, and this relic set is the only relic set that amplifies Elation DMG (20% DEF shred). Additionally, this set also provides 16% CD, which is good for Evanescia given that she particularly wants CD.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit Alternative
For if you don't have any good Magical Girl 4pc.

Planar Ornaments

Punklorde Stage Zero BiS
Best offensive planar for Evanescia, providing 32% CD and 8% Elation.
Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm Alternative
A slightly weaker (3% worse than Punklorde) but good option if you are lacking CR on Evanescia since you are always running Elation allies with her. The ATK boost is minor but does contribute to Evanescia's damage, and the CR is good for lower investment Evanescia builds.

Huohuo
Sustain
Current best team for Evanescia. This team heavily relies on Yao Guang, as she provides a lot of Certified Banger through Aha Instants on Ultimate use, which provide energy for Evanescia, maximising her ult frequency. This team loses 35-40% of its damage if you replace Yao Guang with any generalist support (Sparkle, Tribbie E1, etc). EMC is used as a Certified Banger generator as well and provides a small amount of personal damage, but her main utility is being an Elation unit for Evanescia to obtain Certified Banger from at the moment. EMC can be replaced with Sparxie, but it is only a damage increase if Sparxie E0S1 or higher, or if Evanescia is at E1 or higher. Huohuo provides energy for all allies, but it particularly helps Yao Guang and EMC's ultimate rotations nicely. For better sustain, you can use DHPT or Hyacine instead, but expect a (small but notable) damage loss.

Investment Recommendation

Evanescia E1 > Evanescia S1 = Evanescia E2 > Yao Guang E1 > Yao Guang S1 = Huohuo E1. For a good stopping point, Evanescia E1S1 + Yao Guang E0S0 + Huohuo E0S0 should be a good stopping point and should be more than capable for most endgame content currently.
