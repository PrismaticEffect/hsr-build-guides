Aglaea

"O, noble guest from afar. The wind brought me news of you along the golden threads. My name is Aglaea, the Dressmaster of Okhema and one of Amphoreus' Chrysos Heirs. I hope we treat each other with honesty."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Aglaea is a blast-oriented unit who focuses on taking multiple turns over a short period due to her absurd SPD boost in her enhanced state (activated through her Ultimate). Her memosprite, Garmentmaker, also goes up to ~300 SPD, who can provide additional attacks and is a good chunk of Aglaea's damage profile as well. Her general gameplay loop requires her to maintain her enhanced state by reactivating her Ultimate before her enhanced state expires, though this has been fixed with the inclusion of Cyrene into the team.

Due to her ability to take multiple turns and her general synergy with multiple advancer units (especially Sunday and RMC, alongside other advances like Eagle/DDD), finding the correct build (especially SPD) on Aglaea and her team is quite difficult to optimise and execute in practice. Her and Garmentmaker taking many turns can also result in more efficient damage distribution, which makes optimal execution difficult. As a result, Aglaea is a character with a very low damage floor (though Cyrene or E1 helps with alleviating this) but has a very high damage ceiling.

Build Notes

Recommended stats here assume ATK boots and ATK rope.

CR/CD: Subtract 30% CD if you are using Hero of Triumphant Song and 32% if you are using BananAmusement Park. If you are using Sunday, aim for 80% CR/140% CD instead. If you are using RMC, aim for 90% CR/120% CD instead.

SPD: Subtract 6.1 SPD from Aglaea if you are using Hero of Triumphant Song. The recommended SPD here is for teams involving RMC/Cyrene or Sunday/Cyrene. With Sunday, Aglaea should be 45.9 SPD slower maximum (or 50.4 SPD with S1) out of combat, while Sunday should be 160 SPD minimum. If you are not using Cyrene in the team and you are at E0, use SPD boots and aim for 160+ SPD (and much lower CR/CD than what is listed here, SPD is highest priority). SPD boots with an RMC/Cyrene team can also be used to avoid downtime after Aglaea's first Ultimate.

For more advanced speed tuning with Sunday/Cyrene, refer to this guide here: https://www.reddit.com/r/AglaeaMains/comments/1q0p0nq/updated_speed_tuning_for_the_aglaea_sunday_dhpt/

Rope: ERR rope is preferred for certain fights where more turns is higher priority or if you meet certain damage thresholds with an ERR rope as it is, but the difference between ERR and ATK rope is very situational.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Time Woven Into Gold S1 BiS
Signature light cone, it is the clear BiS for Aglaea as it gives her everything she needs with increased CRIT DMG, Basic ATK DMG, and base SPD (which helps in reaching higher SPD thresholds).
Sweat Now, Cry Less S1-S5 Alternative
Generic BP option for Aglaea as it gives her CR and DMG (both stats that Aglaea wants).
Reminiscence S5 Alternative
For Aglaea, as long as she can keep her Garmentmaker on the field, she is able to maintain full stacks of Reminiscence for the entire fight. Players without sufficient energy regeneration may find that their performance with this cone is worse.
Geniuses' Greetings S5 Alternative
Provides an ATK% buff which Aglaea is extremely oversaturated with, and the uptime of DMG% buff of this LC is quite poor since Aglaea takes multiple turns after using her Ultimate. Used only as a tankier option over Reminiscence S5 in sustainless runs.

Relic Sets

Hero of Triumphant Song BiS
Best set for Aglaea, this offers her and Garmentmaker ATK and CRIT DMG, both stats that she needs to scale and deal damage. The additional SPD also helps with building S0 Aglaea and speed tuning her with Sunday.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit or SPD Alternative
If you do not have a good 4pc Hero of Triumphant Song set, it is sometimes better to mix and match a combination of either 2x CRIT% 2pc sets, or 1x CRIT% 2pc set and 1x SPD% 2pc set, or 2x SPD% 2pc sets.

Planar Ornaments

The Wondrous BananAmusement Park BiS
Provides a total of 48% CD to Aglaea, which is the biggest damage amp provided out of all of the planars. However, due to Garmentmaker being a memosprite, the 32% CRIT DMG bonus doesn't affect it, which is fine since Aglaea herself deals a lot of the damage too.
Rutilant Arena Alternative
Second best planar for Aglaea and very comparable to BananAmusement in performance. Provides 8% CR and a bit of DMG% for Aglaea's damage. Again, like the problem with BananAmusement Park, the DMG% bonus does not affect Garmentmaker.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
The 160 SPD requirement is extremely easy to hit for Aglaea, but again like with all the other options, the DMG% does not affect her memosprite. 2% worse than BananAmusement Park.
Amphoreus, the Eternal Land Alternative
Provides CR (which Aglaea wants) and SPD to all allies (useful for some speed tuning setups), but is otherwise weaker than alternatives like BananAmusement Park or Rutilant Arena.

Investment Recommendation

Aglaea E1 >> Sunday E1 = Aglaea S1 > Cyrene S1 = Sunday S1. Recommended stopping point would be either E1S0 Aglaea + E1S0 Sunday + E0S0 Cyrene, or E1S1 Aglaea + E0S0 Sunday/Cyrene. If you really like Cyrene, you can invest in Cyrene E2 after Aglaea E1 as well.
