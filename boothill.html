Boothill

"Name's Boothill. Those who've heard of me know what I'm about. Those who haven't... well, for the sake of your own skin, you best keep it that way."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Boothill is a single-target Break DPS who revolves around dealing extremely high Break DMG when an enemy is Weakness Broken. He can target an enemy and force himself and the enemy into a Standoff state, where the enemy can only attack Boothill and vice versa. This enables Boothill to use his Enhanced Basic Attack, which deals the bulk of his toughness damage. His Enhanced Basic Attack's toughness damage, however, has a ramp-up time through requiring stacks of Pocket Trickshot, which require the enemy in Standoff to have its toughness bar broken or to be defeated to gain 1 Trickshot stack (for a maximum of 3).

Due to his single-target damage profile and general ramp-up time at E0, Boothill is a fairly skill-intensive break DPS that requires good planning to do well with. In addition, due to his (and the rest of the Break team's) ability to wear defensive main stats, Boothill can be run sustainless fairly comfortably, which considerably raises his damage ceiling.

Build Notes

This build focuses entirely on Boothill's break kit. A large majority (90%+) of Boothill's damage is Break DMG even if you build CR/CD on him, and Break DMG doesn't scale off crit. Thus, it is much better to build BE and SPD on him rather than focusing on crit substats.

SPD: The SPD recommendation allows Boothill to take 3 actions in 150 AV, assuming that you are running The Dahlia (+30% SPD on Boothill) and are using S5 Shadowed by Night or S1 signature, which provides a 12% SPD increase. If you are using Eagle instead of Cavalry, the effective SPD needed with the aforementioned LCs is 138.9 SPD instead. Without Dahlia, aim for 145/160 SPD in combat.

Body/Sphere: Boothill can use any main stat for these slots since none of the main stats increase his damage by much, though HP/DEF main stat is preferred for tankiness.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Sailing Towards a Second Life S1 BiS
Boothill's signature LC and his best LC (about 19-20% better than 4* options), providing him with 60% BE, 12% SPD and 20% DEF shred (very good since his teammates also provide DEF shred).
Shadowed By Night S5 Alternative
Best 4* option as it provides a similar amount of BE as his S1 (56%) and the same SPD increase (12%), but this LC does not have the DEF shred of his signature LC.
Adversarial S5 Alternative
3* LC and F2P option. Gives 18% SPD on enemy kill for 2 turns, but this LC requires consistent kills and does not provide any useful stats beyond that.

Relic Sets

Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge BiS
General BiS relic set for Boothill (10%+ over 2pc/2pc combos), providing him with BE% and DEF shred for his Break DMG (his main source of damage) and Super Break DMG provided by Fugue, Dahlia or HMC.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
A minmax option for Boothill since he actually gets 2-turn Ultimates quite consistently with an enemy kill, increasing his effective SPD by 14.3%, which can potentially give him an extra action at high SPD investment when paired with DDD or Fugue E2. You do lose quite a bit of damage amp from Cavalry, but if you are able to squeeze an extra action with this set, this could outperform Cavalry.
2pc SPD / 2pc BE Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.

Planar Ornaments

Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern BiS
BiS planar if you are using The Dahlia with Boothill, which you should be if you plan on using any break team. Provides the highest BE% boost out of any planar while also providing 6% SPD, which is integral for certain SPD breakpoints with Boothill.
Talia: Kingdom of Banditry Alternative
His second best planar, though it is his BiS planar if you are not using Dahlia. Provides up to 36% BE if you hit 145 SPD, which you should be if you are using Boothill.
