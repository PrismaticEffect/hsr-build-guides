★★★★★

Character Summary

Sparkle is an SP-positive advancer unit that provides ATK, Crit DMG and minor RES PEN to a specific ally and vulnerability to all enemies after the team consumes a certain amount of SP. Given that her action advance is only 50%, her main way of advancing is through advancing a very slow DPS (usually at base SPD, around 99-105 SPD) so that the DPS' effective SPD is equal to Sparkle's SPD. In doing so, however, this requires building Sparkle to a fairly high SPD.

Her Novaflare improving her SP generation and gaining energy from SP consumption solidifies her as the go-to advancer for SP-heavy units like Archer or Sparxie, but due to her improved sources of damage amplification in both her buffs and debuffs, she is an excellent advancer for any DPS who prefers taking a few strong turns rather than multiple weaker turns. She also increases the SP limit of the team, which is especially useful for SP-heavy DPSes.

Build Notes

CD: If you are using A Grounded Ascent (Sunday LC) instead of Earthly Escapade, subtract E0S1's CD recommendation by 32%.

SPD: 160 SPD allows Sparkle (and in turn your DPS) to take 4 actions in 250 AV (2 MoC cycles). For different SPD breakpoints, use the formula (number of turns - total action advance) * 10000 / AV allotted. For example, if you use Vonwacq, want 3 actions (for wave 1) in 150 AV and no further action advance, you need 173.4 SPD. ([3 - 0.4]) * 10000 / 150).

Orb: You can theoretically use any orb for Sparkle as long as it has SPD and CD in its substats, but HP/DEF is the best main stat for tankiness on Sparkle.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Earthly Escapade S1 BiS
Sparkle's LC, and, for any other DPS outside of Archer, this LC provides the best damage amplification out of any other LC Sparkle can use, assuming that you build your DPS around the LC's buffs. Gives your DPS 10% CR and 28% CD, and it also increases Sparkle's CD to increase her CD boost as well. A 16-20% damage amp over Past and Future.
A Grounded Ascent S1 Alternative
Sunday's LC and BiS LC when you are using Sparkle with Archer in particular. Provides good DMG% (though it has some rampup time with Sparkle), SP every other Skill use, and Sparkle gets consistent 2-turn Ultimates with Archer specifically (Sparxie enables 2-turn ultimates with Sparkle without this LC), providing Archer with much more SP per turn than other LCs assuming no hit energy given to Sparkle.
Dance! Dance! Dance! S5 Alternative
Minmaxing option. This LC is excellent if you can squeeze out an extra Sparkle turn (and in turn a DPS action) with this LC as it provides the team with 24% action advance per Ultimate use, but this LC does not provide any damage amplification and Sparkle only gets 3 turn Ultimates with this LC unless she gets a lot of energy from being hit. If you do not get an extra Sparkle turn from using this LC, this LC is worse than all of the LCs on this list, though it is often comparable to premium options if you can get an extra turn.
But the Battle Isn't Over S1 Alternative
Decent LC option that provides ERR for Sparkle (which can lead to some 2-turn Ultimates with hit energy with some DPSes like Archer), 30% DMG for 1 turn to the DPS when Sparkle advances them, and an SP every other Ultimate use.
Past and Future S5 Alternative
F2P option that provides 32% DMG to the DPS for 1 turn when Sparkle advances them.

Relic Sets

Sacerdos' Relived Ordeal BiS
Best damage amplification set for Sparkle as it provides 36% CD to the DPS, and it gives Sparkle 6% SPD, which makes it easier to build Sparkle.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have a good 4pc Sacerdos set.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
Minmaxing option and only beneficial if you can squeeze out an extra turn with this set from the 25% advance per Ultimate use, but this relic set has no damage amplification for your DPS. If you do not get an extra Sparkle turn from using this relic set, this is effectively a set with no useful bonuses for your team, but it can be better than Sacerdos if you can get an extra turn. Building to certain SPD breakpoints with this set is also difficult as you don't get SPD from this set.

Planar Ornaments

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas BiS
Generally good planar, provides 5% ERR for Sparkle (good for Archer teams in particular) and 12% ATK for your DPS.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Provides 5% ERR for Sparkle, and this planar can allow you to get 3 turns in Wave 1 with a lower SPD requirement. Good planar though its utility is more for minmaxing.
Penacony, the Land of Dreams Alternative
Provides 5% ERR for Sparkle, and if you are using a Quantum DPS like Archer, provides a bit of DMG% to them. Theoretically the best damage amplification planar for Archer, and still a great option for Novaflare Seele.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
Comparable damage amp to Lushaka or Penacony for non-Elation teams (BiS for Elation DMG teams). Gives 12% CD to all allies but it requires an enemy kill in doing so., but Sparkle might want the ERR in some teams (like Archer's).
Broken Keel Alternative
Decent planar and one of the best planars for Elation DMG teams. Gives 10% CD to all allies, but requires Sparkle to get 30% Effect RES (which is 3 ERES substats).

Investment Recommendation

For Archer teams, E0S1 (with Sunday's LC) is a great stopping point. For non-Archer teams, E0S0 is already sufficient, but you can aim for E2 if you really like her and use many DPSes that prefer to have Sparkle in their teams.
