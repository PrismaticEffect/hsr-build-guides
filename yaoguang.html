Yao Guang

"I am General Yao Guang, Seer Strategist aboard the Xianzhou Yuque. Like everyone else, you may simply call me Madam Yao. It's true, I could have divined the finer details of this encounter, but where's the fun in that? Being here in person has turned up some rather pleasant surprises."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Yao Guang is an SP-positive Elation-based support which provides general damage amplification through vulnerability, RES PEN, additional Elation DMG procs. However, her main utility is in providing Elation-specific buffs, such as providing Elation (a unique stat that modifies Elation DMG) and triggering an Aha Instant (of 20 Punchline) with her Ultimate, generating a notable amount of Certified Banger for teammates, which is integral for amplifying Elation DMG outside of Elation Skills. Her Elation Skill also provides 1 SP per use, which makes SP economy less straining for Elation teams even with multiple SP negative units.

At the moment, Yao Guang is a cornerstone of all Elation teams due to her quick access to Certified Banger generation (for all Elation DPSes) and Punchline generation for SW999 through her Ultimate, to which all Elation DPSes lose a notable portion (35-50%+) of their damage without Yao Guang. However, outside of Elation teams, Yao Guang's damage amplification and utility falls short of more generalist supports like Tribbie or Cipher, but she has niche uses for teams against enemies that interact with Aha Instants or Elation DMG in general.

Build Notes

CR/CD: Subtract 16% CR if you are using Diviner 4pc and 10% CR if you are using her S1. Prioritise SPD over other stats as it increases the team's overall Elation, though you can build CR on her for some personal damage through her Elation Skill. Yao Guang gains CD through other sources such as her self-buff.

SPD: SPD recommendations are for the closest SPD breakpoints for additional actions within 150-350 AV. To calculate the amount of SPD needed for X actions in Y AV, you can use 10000X / Y to calculate.

Orb: Almost all of her damage is Elation DMG, so no sphere main stat will increase her damage output by much. Use HP/DEF orb for tankiness.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

When She Decided to See S1 BiS
Yao Guang's signature LC and her general best option. Provides 10% CR and 30% CD to the whole team, increases her ERR% (takes 1 fewer turn to generate her Ultimate), and enables her to get a turn 1 Ultimate, which helps with frontloading Punchline for SW999 or Certified Banger for Evanescia. It also allows Yao Guang to be potentially built with 200 SPD, which lets her take 3 actions in 150AV to potentially squeeze in an extra Ultimate.
Mushy Shroomy's Adventures S5 Alternative
Best F2P option, providing a bit of Elation to herself and applying a bit of vulnerability for all Elation DMG (which her teams should be primarily dealing).

Relic Sets

Diviner of Distant Reach BiS
Her best relic set since it provides almost every stat that Yao Guang wants (SPD and CR), and this set provides 10% Elation to all teammates on the first Elation skill as well, which is a small but nice damage amp for the team.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have a Diviner 4pc set. The difference between Diviner 4pc and 2pc SPD/2pc SPD is 10.8% Elation to all allies, which results in about a 3-4% team damage loss for most Elation teams.

Planar Ornaments

Sprightly Vonwacq BiS
General best planar for SW999 teams. Provides 5% ERR (making it a bit easier for her to get her Ultimate in some scenarios) and lets her take action first in Elation teams, which is useful when paired with her S1 for a turn 1 Ultimate.
Lushaka, the Sunken Seas Alternative
General best planar for Sparxie teams and good planar for Evanescia if you want the ERR. Provides 5% ERR and 12% ATK, which makes it easier for Sparxie to maximise her ATK -> Elation conversion. The 12% ATK translates to about 3% CD for Evanescia, so it is usable for her as well if you want the ERR.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
General best planar for Evanescia teams if you don't need the ERR. The ATK boost will not trigger for her, but the team gains 12% CD if an enemy is defeated. Broken Keel can also be used in lieu of this planar for zero ramp-up time and a slight CD loss, but you need to build 20% Effect RES from other sources to activate its secondary CD effect.
Penacony, the Land of Dreams Alternative
Good planar for Evanescia teams if you want the ERR. Provides 5% ERR and 10% DMG to any Physical allies, which translates to about 4-5% CD for Evanescia.

Investment Recommendation

E0S0 Yao Guang is already a completely usable unit for any Elation team, but you can go for E0S1 or E1S0 if you want further investment into her, though I would recommend stopping at this investment and focusing on your Elation DPSes instead until E2S1 for any of the current Elation DPSes.
