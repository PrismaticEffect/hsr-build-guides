Character Summary

Elation MC (or EMC for short) is an Elation support who primarily provides Certified Banger (which affects Elation DMG from Talents of Elation characters), a small amount of Punchline, a small amount of vulnerability and Elation, and, in their Ultimate, they can provide a CD boost to an ally and the ability to copy 1 instance of their Elation Skill. If a non-Elation character was targeted this way, they get advanced by 50% instead.

At the moment, EMC specialises in Elation teams only. EMC's personal damage is fairly low in most teams (other than Evanescia teams), and their damage amplification and utility is much lower than that of other generalist supports like RMC. Regardless, EMC remains a good F2P option for Elation DPSes, though their utility varies considerably based on the Elation DPS and what build EMC is on.

Build Notes

ATK: EMC only needs 4-5 ATK substats to max out their ATK -> Elation conversion with the aforementioned LC. You can use an ATK orb instead to focus more on SPD and crit.

CR/CD: EMC gains 15% CR and 100% CD in combat, so prioritise getting CR over CD.

SPD: You can aim for less SPD and focus more on CR/CD if you like, but EMC does not contribute too much to team damage, so SPD is higher priority. 160 is a general SPD breakpoint, but you can aim for 166.7+ instead.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

When She Decided to See S1 BiS
Yao Guang's signature LC and general best LC for EMC as it provides ERR and SPD for them (both stats that EMC really wants), and it provides a 10% CR/30% CD buff to all allies. If you have multiple copies of this LC, the CR/CD team buff can stack.
Elation Brimming with Blessings S5 Alternative
Best F2P option for EMC as it provides a good amount of ATK (which helps to maximise their ATK -> Elation conversion) and provides a good amount of Elation to a specific ally. The buff uptime of this LC is not 100% with SW999, but it should be at full uptime with either Evanescia or Sparxie.
Mushy Shroomy's Adventures S5 Alternative
Alternative F2P option which provides personal Elation and Elation DMG vulnerability, but it is generally a damage loss compared to Elation Brimming with Blessings in the teams that you would normally use EMC (which are hypercarry rather than dual carry).

Relic Sets

Eagle of Twilight Line BiS
Action-maximising relic set, has a higher SPD increase than 2pc SPD/2pc SPD if you get 2.5 to 3 turn ult rotations (which is possible with Huohuo + an ERR planar), but not much better than 2pc SPD/2pc SPD combos. However, this set can be paired with Vonwacq to get 3 actions in 150 AV if your EMC is at 156+ SPD.
Messenger Traversing Hackerspace Alternative
Marginally best relic set if you are using this in a SW999 team, as it gives SW999 a bit of SPD, which increases her Elation DMG output (even if the buff duration is short). This set also provides 6% SPD, which makes it easier for EMC to hit certain SPD breakpoints.
Sacerdos' Relived Ordeal Alternative
Good set for Evanescia teams since you are running Evanescia fairly slow and EMC fast. Provides up to 36% CD to Evanescia (+8% damage increase) though it requires EMC to apply 2 Ultimates onto Evanescia, which would take about 4-5 EMC turns.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.

Planar Ornaments

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas BiS
Good and efficient to farm planar. Gives EMC 5% ERR (which is needed for some rotations), and the ATK gain can be given to Sparxie in both SW999 teams and Sparxie hypercarry teams or Evanescia for a very small damage boost.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
Efficient to farm planar and general best supportive planar since it provides 12% CD to all allies (which affects Elation DMG), but it requires an enemy kill for the CD effect to trigger. EMC cannot activate the ATK boost of this planar because none of EMC's attacks are follow-ups.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Gives EMC a bit of ERR and good for getting an extra action in Wave 1, but getting an extra action with Vonwacq alone has a fairly high SPD requirement. If you wear this planar, you can aim for 3 actions in the first wave with a 173.4 SPD EMC, or with 156 SPD and 1 Eagle proc.
Broken Keel Alternative
Similar supportive abilities as City of Converging Stars and the supportive buff is unconditional, but EMC needs at least 5-6 Effect RES substats to reach the 30% Effect RES requirement.
