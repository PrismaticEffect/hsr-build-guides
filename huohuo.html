Huohuo

★★★★★

Character Summary

Huohuo is a healer/sustain who provides offensive support to the team, providing ATK to all allies and Energy equal to a proportion of an ally's Max Energy on her Ultimate. Huohuo's healing is split between her Skill and her Talent, the latter of which gives a small amount of healing on an ally's turn or Ultimate use to allies below 50% HP, the ally taking the turn, or the ally with the lowest HP. As a result, Huohuo' sustain is particularly resilient by having very fast teammates or teammates that take many turns.

With her Novaflare, Huohuo has become a slightly SP positive unit (1/3 SP per turn) and her healing capabilities were improved slightly. She also provides a larger ATK buff to units that have 160+ max energy, but this primarily affects Saber and Aglaea. Due to her harder to access emergency healing and lack of effective HP increase, Huohuo is weaker than DHPT or Hyacine in terms of sustain, but her buffs and energy battery makes her useful in many teams, especially if they have high enough damage output to avoid being hit too often.

Build Notes

SPD: Subtract 5.88 SPD if you are using 4pc Warrior Goddess. Ideally, Huohuo should be as fast as possible to proc her emergency healing more, as long as you maintain a healthy balance of SPD and HP substats.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Night of Fright S1 BiS
Huohuo's signature LC and general best LC, but not much better than cheaper alternatives. Provides enough ERR% to ensure a 3-turn Ultimate through spamming Skills, provides a 12 ATK% buff to all allies, and improves her emergency healing.
Shared Feeling S5 Alternative
Best 4* LC if you are spamming skills with Huohuo. Provides a bit of Outgoing Healing boost (good for her sustain) and generates a bit of Energy for all allies every time Huohuo uses a Skill, and this LC also ensures a 3-turn Ultimate with a skill spam rotation.
Post-Op Conversation S5 Alternative
Decent 4* LC for teams where you cannot consume much SP with Huohuo, this LC enables a 4-turn Ultimate rotation while ensuring that Huohuo remains SP positive. However, Huohuo cannot benefit from the Outgoing Healing boost from this LC.
Until Tomorrow's Morrow S5 Alternative
BP option which focuses more on offensive utility; this LC gives a bit of Outgoing Healing boost while also providing a DMG% boost to all allies with 50% or more HP.
Quid Pro Quo S5 Alternative
Decent 4* LC for teams where you cannot consume much SP with Huohuo, this LC gives 16 energy to any ally with less than 50% energy on Huohuo's turn, which might be integral for some allies' rotations. However, this LC does not provide any improvement to her sustain.

Relic Sets

Warrior Goddess of Sky and Thunder BiS
Her best relic set for offensive utility as it provides her with 12% SPD in total and 15% CD to all allies. General best set for crit-scaling teams.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have a good Warrior Goddess 4pc.
Passerby of Wandering Cloud Alternative
A good option since it improves Huohuo's Outgoing Healing and provides 1 SP at the start of combat, which can be useful for some teams like Sparxie teams, but the extra SP is not super useful for most Huohuo teams post-Novaflare.

Planar Ornaments

Broken Keel BiS
General best planar for Elation teams as it gives the entire team 10% CD, and Huohuo only requires 1 ERES substat to hit the 30% Effect RES requirement because of her traces.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
Excellent planar for crit scaling teams and more efficient to farm for Elation teams. Similar to Broken Keel, this planar provides 12% CD to all teammates, but it requires an enemy kill whereas Broken Keel's CD increase is unconditional.
Lushaka, the Sunken Seas Alternative
Good and efficient planar to farm, providing 12% ATK to one character and a small ERR% increase which is useful for getting a comfortable 4-turn Ultimate rotation.
Fleet of the Ageless Alternative
Good planar as it improves Huohuo's healing and provides the entire team with 8% ATK, but this planar is generally worse than Broken Keel outside of triple DoT teams specifically.
