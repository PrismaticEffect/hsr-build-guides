Character Summary

Sparxie is an Elation (sub-)DPS who revolves around heavy SP consumption to accumulate Punchline and deal notable damage from her Enhanced Basic and her Elation Skill. She can offset the heavy SP consumption through her Skill (which can give SP at a low chance), her Ultimate (which gives Thrill, which can be used to offset SP consumption) and her Elation Skill (which also gives Thrill). However, Sparxie cannot sustain her own SP consumption, which requires her to be complemented with SP positive units such as Yao Guang, Sparkle, or Silver Wolf Lv. 999.

Sparxie can be played as her own hypercarry DPS, but due to her overall supportive capabilities (e.g. providing CD to all allies) and her general synergy with other Elation allies, she can also be played with other Elation DPSes such as Silver Wolf Lv.999 or Evanescia (though the former has better synergy with her than the latter).

Build Notes

"ATK: If you are using SPD boots, hitting 3600 ATK is not a high priority. Aim for as high ATK as possible while still maintaining high CR/CD. With ATK boots and Sparkle, hitting 3600 ATK should be much more doable.

CR/CD: Prioritise CR over CD as Sparxie has many CD sources (Tengoku 2pc, Sparkle, and her 80% CD self-buff) and very few CR sources without premium options. If you have additional CR sources, reduce the recommended CR stat listed here accordingly.

SPD: Only aim for 107 SPD if you are using Sparxie as a hypercarry with Sparkle. ""Faster than Aha"" refers to her taking action before natural Aha instants to maximise Punchline per Aha instant."

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Dazzled by a Flowery World S1 BiS
Sparxie's signature LC and her unconditional BiS LC. Provides 48% CD to herself, increases her SP limit by 2-3 (which maximises her Punchline generation and EBA damage), 20% DEF shred to her Elation DMG, and provides 20% Elation to all allies. About a 15-16% team damage improvement over F2P options for sub-DPS Sparxie and a 22%+ damage improvement for hypercarry Sparxie.
Today's Good Luck S3+ Alternative
BP option that provides 12-20% CR (great for making her easier to build) and personal Elation% to herself, but does not provide any overall team damage amplification, which makes this only comparable to F2P options. This is 3-5% better than the F2P option (Mushy Shroomy's Adventures) in a hypercarry Sparxie team, however.
Mushy Shroomy's Adventures S5 Alternative
Best F2P option, providing a bit of Elation to herself and applying a bit of vulnerability for all Elation DMG (which her teams should be primarily dealing).

Relic Sets

Ever-Glorious Magical Girl BiS
Her BiS relic set with very few alternatives (8% better than 2pc combos). Almost all of Sparxie's damage is Elation DMG, and this relic set is the only relic set that amplifies Elation DMG (20% DEF shred). Additionally, this set also provides 16% CD.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit Alternative
For if you don't have any good Magical Girl 4pc.

Planar Ornaments

Tengoku@Livestream BiS
Strongest offensive planar for Sparxie, provides a total of 48% CD (since Sparxie can easliy meet the 3 SP consumed requirement of this planar).
Punklorde Stage Zero Alternative
Very comparable alternative to Tengoku@Livestream if you have farmed more Punklorde pieces than you have farmed Tengoku. Provides 8% Elation and 32% CD, which is on par with Tengoku@Livestream for both hypercarry and sub-DPS Sparxie.
Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm Alternative
A slightly weaker (3% worse than Punklorde) but good option if you are lacking CR on Sparxie since you are always running Elation allies with her. The ATK boost is minor but does contribute to Sparxie's ATK -> Elation conversion if you are running her as a sub-DPS.
