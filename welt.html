Character Summary

Welt is a debuffer who can function both as a DPS or a support depending on how you build him. He provides a very substantial (40%) DEF shred debuff, a DMG% buff, and at higher eidolons, 30% RES down. He also reduces enemy SPD and can delay enemies, which gives him slightly better defensive capabilities than other DPSes or even supports if enemies are not able to take many turns from his delay and slow.

While Welt has very notable damage multipliers in his kit through his Skill and Talent, he has a very steep EHR requirement alongside having to build CR/CD on him, which makes building Welt as a DPS extremely difficult without his E6, usually requiring 28-32 substats for him and specific supports like Sunday to be a viable DPS. He is easier to build as a support, but his damage amplification is better than other supports only at E4, though his delay+slow utility can help with reducing damage taken in sustainless runs at E2 or higher.

Build Notes

Recommended build is for hypercarry Welt. Recommended stats assume that you are running Sunday in this team, as Welt requires too many substats to reach all of his recommended stats + 100% CR reliably by himself. If you are running Welt as a pure support, focus on hitting 80% EHR + as much SPD as possible.

EHR: 66.7% is the minimum EHR required to land Weightless against 40% ERES enemies. However, you can aim for 80% to maximise his EHR to ATK conversion, but it is not needed. If you are using Welt's signature LC, you can aim for lower EHR (about 50% EHR) in exchange for more crit substats.

CR/CD: Subtract 4% CR/24% CD if you are using Pioneer 4pc.

SPD: If you are using Sparkle + Sunday, use ATK boots and base speed. If you are using Sunday only, aim for 133.34 SPD. If you are running Welt as purely a support, aim for as fast as possible while ignoring all crit recommendations.

Rope: If your Welt is E2, you can use ERR rope for 2-turn Ultimates, but this is more for a support Welt than a DPS Welt build.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Incessant Rain S1 BiS
Silver Wolf's signature LC and most comfortable LC option for building hypercarry Welt. Provides unconditional EHR and CR (both stats that Welt really wants), and while the vulnerability debuff of this LC is single target and has poor uptime, it can be applied frequently and is a small damage amp for the team.
Along the Passing Shore S1 Alternative
Acheron's signature LC, and theoretical best LC for Welt for high relic investment Welt builds (66.7-80% EHR with 20+ crit substats). Provides CD% and DMG%, but it does not provide any stats that makes Welt easier to build, which means that this LC can perform worse than other LCs below this if Welt does not have enough EHR or CR in particular.
In the Name of the World S1+ Alternative
Welt's signature LC and makes Welt easier to build. Provides DMG% and ATK%, and while this LC only provides EHR for his Skill only (which does not convert to additional ATK% from his A6), it allows you to get away with lower EHR on Welt (60% or less) since he is able to inflict Slow on his Skill more easily (needed for his additional DMG procs), making him less reliant on his Ultimate to inflict Slow.
Boundless Choreo S5 Alternative
Best 4* option as it provides 16% CR and 48% CD, which allows you to focus much more on building EHR instead.
Resolution Shines as Pearls of Sweat S5 Alternative
Best 4* option for a pure support Welt; Cipher's signature LC has the best damage amp for support Welt.

Relic Sets

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters BiS
His general best set as he can inflict 3 debuffs consistently and provides DMG% and CR/CD which he generally lacks.
Wastelander of Banditry Desert Alternative
Similar damage amp to Pioneer 4pc (10% DMG, 10% CR and 20% CD), but this set has a harder secondary effect to trigger as it requires enemies to be Imprisoned, which is more difficult to do against certain enemies in endgame content. Regardless, this is still a great set if you have pieces from this back in 1.x.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
For a purely support Welt build. At E2 + ERR rope, he gets 2-turn Ultimates, which allows Welt to get an effective 14.3% total SPD increase from this set.

Planar Ornaments

Pan-Cosmic Commercial Enterprise BiS
Best unconditional planar for Welt since it provides 10% EHR and a bit of ATK% for all of his attacks. No other planar is unconditionally good for him.
Rutilant Arena Alternative
Provides 8% CR, but the Skill DMG% increase is not very significant since a bit more than half of his DMG is from his additional DMG procs from his Talent.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
Conditionally good planar on Welt if you are building speed on Welt. Provides 12% ATK and 12% DMG if he reaches 135+ SPD, but otherwise it is only a 12% ATK planar without any SPD on him.
The Wondrous BananAmusement Park Alternative
Theoretical best damage amplification planar for Welt (48% CD), but it locks you to using DHPT in the team.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
For a support Welt build. The 40% advance at the start of Wave 1 can be used to delay faster enemies earlier to avoid damage taken.

Investment Recommendation

For his supports, Sunday S1 > Support E0S1/E1S0 (or E2 Sparkle) = DHPT E1. Welt E4 and E6 are larger improvements than Sunday S1. E2S1 Welt, E0S1 Sunday and E0S1 Sparkle, E1S0 Cerydra or E0S1 Ashveil would be the minimum investment required for him to be used as a DPS for harder endgame content.
