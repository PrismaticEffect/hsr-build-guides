Character Summary

Seele is a single-target Skill-based DPS unit that revolves around her Resurgence mechanic, which gives her an extra turn and greatly increases her DMG% on an enemy kill through her Skill, Basic ATK or Ultimate. While Seele cannot retrigger Resurgence if Seele defeats enemies with an attack during her Resurgence turn, Seele can still generate up to 2-3 extra turns per natural turn if played most ideally with a skill duplicating unit like Cerydra.

Her Novaflare solidifies her extra turn on enemy kill gimmick by now giving her a notable DMG boost every time she defeats an enemy, and she can now trigger an additional Skill use if an enemy is below 50% HP, which is another potential Resurgence trigger. Thus, Seele excels in scenarios where there are many small enemies to defeat like in Pure Fiction or specific bosses like Flame Reaver or Nikador, but Seele struggles in scenarios where there are few to no small enemies to kill to trigger Resurgence or gain a substantial DMG% boost from.

Build Notes

SPD: Seele reaches 160+ SPD in combat with ATK boots and no SPD substats when paired with Cerydra. If not using Cerydra, you can aim for 121.25+ or 131.25+ SPD to aim for 160 SPD if you are using Seele's signature LC, but it is not recommended.

Orb: In her best team (Sparkle/Cerydra/DHPT) which has a lot of ATK sources, DMG orb is slightly (2%) better (and better in earlier parts of the fight), but ATK orb is still comparable and is better in other teams.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

The Hell Where Ideals Burn S1 BiS
Archer's signature LC and Seele's BiS LC if you are using Sparkle due to the LC's notable (80%) ATK boost on all attacks. This LC does have a bit of ramp-up time and it requires Seele to consume 4 SP to reach its maximum effect, but this LC already outperforms Seele's signature after 1-2 attacks. About 12% better than Cruising assuming only the ATK boost from Cruising.
In The Night S1 Alternative
Seele's signature LC and her best cone outside of Sparkle teams. Provides DMG% to her Basic ATK and Skill and CD to her Ultimate, but both of those stats are notably saturated on most of her teams. This LC also requires 160 SPD to take full effect, which requires Cerydra, Seele E2 or a notable amount of SPD substats for the LC to gain its maximum effect. About a 10% increase over Cruising.
Cruising in the Stellar Sea S5 Alternative
Best F2P option. Seele can often maintain 75%+ uptime on the secondary ATK boost of this LC assuming that Seele kills an enemy every 2-3 turns, which is something that you should be doing with Seele in the first place. You can build around the secondary 16% CR buff of this LC, but it makes runs much less consistent and is not recommended.
See You at the End S5 Alternative
BP option; provides a larger crit boost to Seele than Cruising and increases Seele's Skill DMG by 40%, and this LC at S5 can be better than Cruising S5 assuming few enemy kills, but you ideally should be maintaining a consistent stream of kills with Seele anyway, which makes Cruising a generally better option in the scenarios where you should be using Seele in the first place.

Relic Sets

Scholar Lost in Erudition BiS
Generally good set and efficient to farm as it provides CR and a good amount of DMG% for her Skill and Ultimate (most of her damage). Comparable performance to Genius if Seele is against non-Quantum weak enemies and with no DEF shred sources, but otherwise a ~4% damage loss from Genius.
Genius of Brilliant Stars Alternative
Conditional BiS relic set for Seele and it provides DMG% to all of her attacks and 10-20% DEF shred depending on the weakness type of enemies. The performance of this set improves with E1, additional DEF shred sources or Quantum weakness. Without any of these aforementioned sources, however, Scholar Lost in Erudition is generally comparable to this set.
Wavestrider Captain Alternative
Conditional BiS relic set as Seele can maintain the 48% ATK buff of this set quite well with enough Resurgence triggers, but it requires many enemy kills for this set to be reliable. With no DEF shred sources, Wavestrider is 4-5% better than Scholar or Genius at 75% uptime of its ATK boost, and comparable to Genius with more DEF shred sources. However, this set falls short of both sets if you can't trigger Resurgence often enough.
2pc Crit / 2pc Crit Alternative
For if you don't have any of the above 4pcs.

Planar Ornaments

Rutilant Arena BiS
General best planar for Seele; provides 8% CR and a 20% DMG boost to her Skill and Basic (notably not her Ultimate).
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
Comparable performance to Rutilant Arena (and can outperform Rutilant in some situations) as it provides a bit of ATK and 18% DMG to all of her attacks, but it requires Seele to be at 160 SPD, which requires Cerydra, E2 or some SPD on Seele.
The Wondrous BananAmusement Park Alternative
Good planar that gives 48% CD to Seele, but it locks you into using DHPT.

Investment Recommendation

E0S1 Seele is already a competent enough DPS for Pure Fiction-like scenarios, but if you wish to invest into Seele further, you can look into Cerydra E1 (or S1 if DDD does not give you much), Sparkle E2 or DHPT E1.
