★★★★★

Character Summary

Lingsha is a sustain who provides healing and debuff cleansing through her AoE skill and her summon (Fuyuan), which does AoE follow-up attacks. Lingsha also has specific synergies with break teams as her Ultimate provides a 2-turn debuff that increases vulnerability for all Break DMG, which is one of the few modifiers that affect overall Break DMG.

Due to Lingsha's high-frequency attacks and notable AoE toughness damage, Lingsha herself can be played as a break DPS in multiple-target fights while maintaining a lot of survivability, though it likely requires higher investment on both her and her break supports to reach comparable performance to more specialised break DPSes. Lingsha also synergises well with most teams that wants high frequency attacks, such as Feixiao or Ashveil teams. While Lingsha is the strongest sustain for break teams, due to the inherent defensive capabilities of breaking enemies in general (action delay), sustains are generally not used in break teams if the fight is short enough. However, in longer fights, Lingsha is generally the premium option for those teams.

Build Notes

BE: Subtract 40% BE if you are using Kalpagni 2pc. With The Dahlia or HMC/Fugue, you should be able to hit 200% BE in combat easily, so BE is not a very high priority outside of using Lingsha DPS.

SPD: 150 SPD ensures that Fuyuan takes 2 turns every 3 natural Lingsha turns without LIngsha's Ultimate, but you can aim for higher SPD if you have a frequent DDD user or Fugue E2.

Rope: If you are using Night of Fright S1, Post-Op Conversation S5 or Dream's Montage S5, use a BE rope instead of ERR rope. Otherwise, you should use a ERR rope unless stated otherwise in the recommended LC descriptions.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Scent Alone Stays True S1 BiS
Her signature LC and usually her best LC for most teams. Provides Lingsha with 60% BE and the team with 18% vulnerability, though the latter's uptime can be limited in non-break teams.
Echoes of the Coffin S1 Alternative
Conditional BiS LC, particularly for DPS Lingsha teams. You can get 2-turn Ultimates with this LC easily (requires 2+ enemies), which might provide enough toughness damage (and in turn Super Break damage) which in turn can lead to the highest potential damage output overall. 2-turn Ultimates also guarantee full uptime on her break vulnerability debuff.
Post-Op Conversation S5 Alternative
Best unconditional 4* LC for sustain and break damage, unless you have a really good ERR rope (in which case, use What Is Real? S5). Enables a 3-turn Ultimate SBB rotation with a BE rope instead of an ERR rope, which can lead to easier stat-building on Lingsha overall. You can use S3-S4 instead if you have a 5% ERR planar.
Dream's Montage S5 Alternative
Provides 12% SPD to Lingsha and allows Lingsha to wear a BE rope instead of ERR rope while still maintaining a 3-turn Ultimate SBB rotation if Lingsha or Fuyuan attacks a Weakness Broken enemy 3 times per Ultimate. Theoretically better than Post-Op Conversation S5 if you can ensure consistent 3t Ultimates, but Post-Op Conversation is generally more consistent.
Quid Pro Quo S5 Alternative
General utility LC, provides energy for other allies on her turn.

Relic Sets

2pc SPD / 2pc SPD BiS
Easiest to build relic set that helps Lingsha hit certain SPD breakpoints more easily. Alternative 2pcs you can use include 2pcs that increase Break Effect like Watchmaker or Iron Cavalry, but 2pc SPD is the most valuable for Lingsha generally.
Warrior Goddess of Sky and Thunder Alternative
General best 4pc for any team that scales off CR/CD (any team that isn't break or DoT), as it provides 15% CD to all allies. This set also provides 12% SPD to Lingsha.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
Theoretically provides the highest SPD increase for Lingsha (+9.1% total SPD), but it makes Lingsha quite difficult to build overall. The SPD increase goes up to 14.3% with Echoes of the Coffin S1.
Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge Alternative
Best 4pc if you are building a more Super Break-oriented Lingsha build.

Planar Ornaments

Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern BiS
Provides Lingsha with 6% SPD and 40% BE (you are almost always using Dahlia in a team with Lingsha which implants Fire weakness), both of which are beneficial stats for using her as a pure sustain or a DPS.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Any 5% ERR planar would fit here, but Vonwacq or Lushaka generally provides the most utility for break teams depending on the team. Lushaka helps improve Firefly's damage output slightly and helps Rappa maximise her ATK -> vulnerability threshold if not using Rappa's S1. Vonwacq helps with making her a bit more SP positive in Wave 1 as she can take 2 actions before Fuyuan, which allows her to use a Basic at the start of the wave instead of a Skill.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
The best offensive planar for crit-scaling teams, as it provides a bit of ATK to Lingsha (for healing) and provides the team with 12% CD if an enemy is defeated. Broken Keel has comparable performance but requires Lingsha to be built with 30% Effect RES.

Investment Recommendation

E0S0 Lingsha is already decent enough as a sustain for break teams and should not require any further vertical investment, but you can look into E1S0 Lingsha for break teams if you are planning on using her in break teams with a sustain. As a break DPS, an E1S0 Lingsha with E0S1/E1S0 Dahlia and E1S0/E0S1 Fugue is a good enough core for most high-target stages.
