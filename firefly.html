Firefly

"Fyreflies are such magical creatures, aren't they? They may throw themselves at a flame or suddenly grow old, but every night before that, they will shine brighter than the stars."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Firefly is a blast-oriented Break DPS who relies on her Ultimate to enter her enhanced state, which notably increases her SPD and allows her to use Enhanced Skills to deal large amounts of toughness damage, which converts to Super Break damage (though without The Dahlia, she can only inflict damage when enemies are weakness broken). She can also implant Fire weakness on all enemies that she attacks post-Novaflare, which makes her usable regardless of weakness typing.

Her Novaflare further lowers her Break Effect requirements, which makes her easier to build SPD on her than before. Additionally, the duration of her enhanced state is extended by up to 30% if she breaks 3 enemies, though the extension is removed during AV resets between resets, which is only relevant in wave-related endgame content like Memory of Chaos.

Build Notes

BE: Due to her A6, an ATK substat roughly converts to about 0.5 BE substats, so aim for BE and ATK substats in your relics, but BE should be higher priority.

SPD: The SPD breakpoints are for teams with The Dahlia, ensuring 4 enhanced actions within 150 AV while minimising FF downtime. DDD refers to Dance! Dance! Dance!, but these procs can also refer to Fugue E2's ult advance as well. You can go for lower than the speed ranges recommended if not 0-cycling. For Firefly users with Phainon's LC, aim for 158.9-168.3 SPD (1 DDD proc) or 178.3-188.8 SPD (0 DDD procs).

Assuming a full 30% delay on her Enhanced state (more relevant in AS, PF and AA), to ensure 5 actions within 150 AV + minimum downtime, you can aim for 159.6 - 165.1 SPD with 1 DDD proc or 175.6 - 178.0 SPD with 0 DDD procs.

Without The Dahlia, aim for either 150 SPD or 164.34 SPD out of combat instead, while subtracting 10.4 SPD if you are using Ruan Mei.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Whereabouts Should Dreams Rest S1 BiS
Firefly's signature LC which provides 60% BE, 24% break vulnerability and an SPD reduction on enemies. Minimum 10% damage increase over Aeon, but the performance of that LC can notably increase against tankier enemies and Firefly is able to squeeze in an extra turn through the Slow effect of her signature LC.
Thus Burns the Dawn S1 Alternative
Phainon's signature LC and most comfortable premium option. Provides a very high base ATK LC (which is useful since she has an ATK -> BE conversion), 12 base SPD (which increases the amount of SPD Firefly gets from Kalpagni and Dahlia) and 18% DEF shred. Generally leads to a 15% damage increase if Firefly's build is built around this LC's speed increase.
On the Fall of an Aeon S5 Alternative
F2P option, provides 64% ATK which converts to about 56-60% BE, making this a great stat stick for Firefly overall.

Relic Sets

Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge BiS
General BiS relic set for Firefly, providing her with BE% and DEF shred for her Super Break DMG (her main source of damage) provided by herself, Fugue, Dahlia or HMC.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD or BE Alternative
For if you don't have a good 4pc Iron Cavalry set, but the difference between Iron Cavalry and 2pc combos is notable enough where even a mediocre Iron Cavalry 4pc would beat very good 2pc combos.

Planar Ornaments

Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern BiS
Unconditional BiS planar since you are always implanting a Fire weakness with Firefly. Provides the highest BE% boost (40%) out of any planar while also providing 6% SPD, which is integral for certain SPD breakpoints with Firefly.
Talia, Kingdom of Banditry Alternative
Her second best planar as it provides 36% BE which is the only offensive stat she scales off (you are always hitting the 145 SPD requirement on Firefly).

Investment Recommendation

Dahlia S1 > Dahlia E2 = Firefly E2 > Firefly/Phainon S1 = Fugue E1 = Fugue S1 > Fugue E2. An E0S0 Firefly with E0S0 Dahlia and E0S0 Fugue is already a competent enough team, but you can go for E2 on either Firefly or Dahlia to make the team much stronger in endgame content. S1 on Dahlia or Fugue is also a cheaper but still notable damage increase for Firefly teams.
