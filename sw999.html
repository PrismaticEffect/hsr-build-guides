Silver Wolf LV.999

★★★★★

Character Summary

Silver Wolf LV.999 is a SPD-scaling and Elation DMG DPS. Her kit is based around entering her Enhanced state through her Ultimate, which she accumulates energy (called Hidden MMR) for through Punchline generation. Upon entering her Enhanced state, her CR/CD is greatly enhanced as Silver Wolf gains more HIdden MMR (through generating more Punchline or using Elation Skills with 20/40+ Punchline) during her enhanced state, and the strength of her EBAs are also enhanced past certain Punchline thresholds.

Build Notes

CR/CD: SW999 gains a lot of CR/CD in her enhanced state (from her Ultimate), so building CR/CD is slightly lower priority than SPD. However, it's recommended to get as close as to 100% CR she would reach 100% CR as soon as possible to maximise the damage of her first EBA. Sparxie also provides a good amount of CD for SW999.

SPD: Other than 200 SPD, there are no particularly notable breakpoints for SW for 0-2 cycle fights. SPD recommendations are for the closest SPD breakpoints for additional actions within 150-350 AV. To calculate the amount of SPD needed for X actions in Y AV, you can use 10000X / Y to calculate.

Orb/Rope: Since SW999 doesn't scale off any offensive stat or need Energy, she can use any main stat orb and rope. HP/DEF main stats are preferred for survivability, but use orbs/ropes that give the best substats.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Welcome to the Cosmic City S1 BiS
Her signature LC and her general best option. About a 12-14% team damage increase (30% personal increase), but the LC makes it easier to build SW and for her to reach 200 SPD, which allows her to take 3 actions within 150 AV or 6 actions within 300 AV. If she takes 3 actions within 150 AV, the team damage improvement over F2P options is closer to 35-40%.
Mushy Shroomy's Adventures S5 Alternative
Best F2P option that increases her Elation and provides Elation DMG vulnerability for all allies.
Today's Good Luck S5 Alternative
BP option which provides CR and personal Elation, but this LC is only better than Mushy Shroomy at S3 or if you are using EMC instead of Sparxie on the team, since this LC provides more personal damage amp while Mushy provides team damage amp.

Relic Sets

Ever-Glorious Magical Girl BiS
Her BiS relic set. All of SW999's damage is Elation DMG, and this relic set is the only relic set that amplifies Elation DMG (20% DEF shred). Additionally, this set also provides 16% CD, which also scales off Elation DMG.
Diviner of Distant Reach Alternative
Her second best relic set as it provides notable buffs that SW999 wants (18% CR and 6% SPD), but the secondary effect of providing Elation to all allies is wasted since Yao Guang should be wearing this set, and multiple effects of this set do not stack. 3% worse than Magical Girl in terms of team damage, but this set can be better than Magical Girl if this set lets you hit 200 SPD while Magical Girl cannot.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have any 4pc sets for her. 7-8% damage loss from Magical Girl.

Planar Ornaments

Punklorde Stage Zero BiS
Her BiS planar as it provides the best unconditional offensive buffs for SW999 (8% Elation and 32% CD, both of which scale off Elation DMG).
Izumo Gensei and Divine Takama Realm Alternative
Decent but weaker alternative planar (4-5% worse than Punklorde) as she is always using Elation teammates with her. Provides 12% CR for SW999, but the ATK boost is wasted as all of her damage is Elation DMG.

SW999's current best team. This team heavily relies on Yao Guang as she is the only ally who readily provides teamwide Aha Instants, which is integral for Punchline generation and, in turn, Hidden MMR generation. Investment Recommendation

S1 SW > S1 Sparxie > S1 Yao Guang > E1 SW = E1 Yao Guang = E1 Sparxie > E2 SW > E2 Sparxie. However, a team of E0S1 SW, E0S1 Sparxie, E0S0 Yao Guang and E0S0 Huohuo is more than competitive for most types of endgame content in the game as of 4.2. If you really like Sparxie or SW, go for their E2s first after E0S1, as it technically provides the best improvement per premium pull compared to any other investment.
