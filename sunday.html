Sunday

"Every reunion is also a new encounter. Hello, I am "Sunday," a humble passenger."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Sunday is an SP light advancer unit that provides CR, CD, and a bit of DMG. His Ultimate also provides energy for a specific ally. He gives an additional DMG boost to any unit with a summon (Jing Yuan, Topaz, Lingsha, or any Remembrance unit), which can also be activated by running DHPT with Sunday and assigning a designated DPS as DHPT's Bondmate.

Sunday overall provides a decent floor for most hypercarry DPSes who do not have dedicated supports. Sunday's CR/CD buffs greatly help with easing relic requirements on hypercarry DPSes, and his lax relic requirements for most teams make him easy to build. The main requirement is that the DPS should be faster than Sunday to take full advantage of his 100% action advance (also known as a -1 SPD setup). While it is possible to run a fast Sunday (160+ SPD) and a slow DPS, it is generally not recommended as it wastes a good amount of Sunday's action advance (and Sparkle has better buffs for this setup), though you can use this setup if you are pairing him with Sparkle.

Build Notes

SPD: The SPD recommendation here is for a -1 SPD Sunday setup, which involves your DPS taking 2 turns in 1 rotation (DPS -> Sunday -> DPS). You can use a hyperspeed Sunday setup (160 SPD Sunday with 190% CD and base speed DPS), but this is only recommended for Saber or Aglaea teams (or any team paired with Sparkle), it is usually a damage loss in other teams.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

A Grounded Ascent S1 BiS
His signature LC and his general best LC option. Enables a consistent 3-turn Ultimate rotation, provides 45% DMG to an ally, and refunds an SP on every 2nd Skill or Ultimate use that targets an ally, making Sunday a fairly SP positive unit. This LC is also BiS for Sparkle in Archer teams.
But the Battle Isn't Over S1+ Alternative
Decent LC option that provides ERR for Sunday (which can lead to some 3-turn Ultimates with hit energy), 30% DMG for 1 turn to the DPS when Sunday advances them, and an SP every other Ultimate use. S3 of this LC also enables a consistent 3-turn Ultimate rotation as long as Sunday is wearing an ERR planar like Lushaka.
Dance! Dance! Dance! S5 Alternative
Minmaxing option but generally unreliable on Sunday. This LC is excellent if you can squeeze out an extra DPS rotation with this LC as it provides the team with 24% action advance per Ultimate use, but this LC does not provide any damage amplification and Sunday only gets 4 turn Ultimates with this LC unless he gets energy from being hit. If you do not get an extra DPS rotation from using this LC, this LC is worse than all of the LCs on this list, though it is better than Past and Future or BTBIO S1 if you can get an extra rotation.
Past and Future S5 Alternative
F2P option that provides 32% DMG to the DPS for 1 turn when Sunday advances them.

Relic Sets

Sacerdos' Relived Ordeal BiS
Best damage amplification set for Sunday by far as it provides 36% CD to the DPS (and the ramp-up is almost zero with Sunday), and it gives Sunday 6% SPD, which makes it easier to build Sunday.
2pc SPD / 2pc SPD Alternative
For if you don't have a good Sacerdos set.
Eagle of Twilight Line Alternative
Minmaxing option that is only usable for hyperspeed Sunday setups, and even then it is quite situational. Minmaxing option and only beneficial if you can squeeze out an extra turn with this set from the 25% advance per Ultimate use, but this relic set has no damage amplification for your DPS. If you do not get an extra Sunday turn from using this relic set, this is effectively a set with no useful bonuses for your team, but it can be better than Sacerdos if you can get an extra turn. Building to certain SPD breakpoints with this set is also difficult as you don't get SPD from this set. Only use this if you know what you are doing.

Planar Ornaments

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas BiS
Generally good planar and efficient to farm, provides 5% ERR for Sunday (good for achieving 3-turn Ultimates with some LCs) and 12% ATK for your DPS.
Broken Keel Alternative
Better offensive buffs for both your DPS and your team than Lushaka (10% CD), and Sunday only needs 1 ERES substat to reach the 30% Effect RES requirement. However, the ERR% on Lushaka might be useful for some Sunday setups.
City of Converging Stars Alternative
Similar to Broken Keel, this planar provides 12% CD to all teammates, but it requires an enemy kill whereas Broken Keel's CD increase is unconditional.
Sprightly Vonwacq Alternative
Strictly used for hyperspeed Sunday. Provides 5% ERR for Sunday, and this planar can allow you to get 3 turns in Wave 1 with a lower SPD requirement. Good planar though its utility is more for minmaxing.

Investment Recommendation

Sunday greatly benefits from his S1 for most teams, so you can stop at E0S1 Sunday and invest into other teammates. If you are using Sunday with Aglaea, you can aim for E1S0 instead. Investing further into him is generally not recommended outside of E6 (and that's if you really like him).
