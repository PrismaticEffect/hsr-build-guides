Hysilens

"Helektra? That name has long since sunk into the depths of the past... Ah, it's you, little gray fry. Call me Hysilens. I am the Knight Commander of the holy city, and I will remain on watch. I will not let the storms of fate cloud your eyes."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Hysilens is a DoT-based unit that deals DoT damage through a zone deployed by her Ultimate. The zone's DoT damage is based on the number of DoTs inflicted onto an enemy (which Hysilens herself provides).

While her Ultimate has a built-in detonation, she is still heavily reliant on detonators like Kafka and additional DoT sources like Black Swan to maximise her damage output.

Build Notes

Prisoner 4pc and Revelry 2pc are assumed for these stats. If you are using Pan-Cosmic instead, subtract the ATK recommendations by 290 at S0 and 330 at S1.

120% EHR for Hysilens is basically a requirement due to her A2 trace, not a suggestion. Beyond 120% EHR, focus more on ATK or SPD.

ATK: Recommended stats assume 2 and 3 ATK main stats. Reduce ATK recommendation by 500 if you are aiming for 160 SPD.

SPD: 160+ SPD Hysilens is only recommended for higher relic investment, or you can focus more on EHR and using an ATK body instead. A higher SPD Hysilens is more useful in modes like Pure Fiction or Apocalyptic Shadow, since enemies spawn there more frequently and Hysilens might need to attack more often to apply her Skill.

If you are using Hysilens S1 on either Swan or Hysilens, you can aim for 128.5 SPD or 152.5 SPD instead for an effective 133.34/160 SPD Hysilens in wave 1 of MoC.

Orb: DMG is 2-3% stronger in very short fights, but ATK is comparable to DMG in longer fights. ATK is better than DMG at E1 and long fights.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Why Does The Ocean Sing S1 BiS
Her signature LC and her best unconditional LC at E0 level. Provides DoT vulnerability to all enemies on debuff application and increases the SPD of all units that attack an enemy with the LC's debuff, which makes building DoT significantly easier.
Those Many Springs S1 Alternative
Jiaoqiu's signature LC and second best LC for triple DoT at E0 level. Provides a massive 60% EHR boost which makes it more feasible to use an ATK body instead of an EHR body to improve Hysilens' offensive stats. This LC also provides a 24% vulnerability debuff, but it requires Hysilens to attack enemies to inflict the debuff, which can be difficult to do against bosses with summons or phase changes.
Reforged Remembrance S1 Alternative
Black Swan's signature LC and second best LC for dual DoT at E0/E1 level. Hysilens can make use of this LC since she applies all four DoT types, and it provides ATK and DEF shred to Hysilens' personal damage. Works better for dual DoT than in triple DoT since this only affects Hysilens' personal damage, and performs worse than the above 2 LCs in triple DoT. At E1S1 Swan and Hysilens, you can swap each other's signature LCs for a potential damage increase if enemies are Physical weak or are summoned frequently like in Pure Fiction (since Hysilens' DoT damage have less rampup time than Swan).
Eyes of the Prey S5 Alternative
General best 4* option as it provides EHR and DMG (both stats that Hysilens really wants), about a 16% damage loss from her signature LC, but the difference can be much higher (up to 30%) if you don't reach specific SPD breakpoints with Kafka (which Hysilens' signature LC makes much easier to achieve).
Solitary Healing S5 Alternative
A niche option for Pure Fiction-like fights since this LC generates energy for every enemy defeated; she can usually get 1-turn Ultimates with enough kills with this LC, but building 120% EHR with this LC is extremely difficult (17 substats) and is not recommended without a high investment Hysilens build.

Relic Sets

Prisoner in Deep Confinement BiS
Dedicated DoT set and overall BiS relic set for Hysilens. Set provides ATK and DEF shred which Hysilens wants.
ATK 2pc / DMG 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have a Prisoner 4pc, but it is highly recommended to farm Prisoner if you plan on running a DoT team in general.

Planar Ornaments

Revelry by the Sea BiS
Strongest unconditional DoT planar, providing 12% ATK and 24% DoT DMG.
Firmament Frontline: Glamoth Alternative
A good alternative if you do not have Revelry pieces since it provides 12% ATK and some DMG, but it requires Hysilens to be built with at least 135 SPD (for +12% DMG), and 160 SPD for the maximum DMG boost (18%) from this planar. In triple DoT, Revelry by the Sea is a 2% improvement over Glamoth (135 SPD) and Pan-Cosmic. The difference is less than 1.5% for Glamoth (160 SPD).
Pan-Cosmic Commercial Enterprise Alternative
A good alternative if you do not have Revelry pieces, providing 10% EHR and 25% ATK to Hysilens. But due to Kafka giving 100% ATK to Hysilens, this planar ends up performing slightly worse than Revelry in DoT teams but comparable to Glamoth at 135 SPD.

Hysilens E1 > Hysilens S1 = Black Swan E1 > Kafka E1 > Black Swan/Jiaoqiu S1. A team of Kafka E0S0, Hysilens E1S1 and Black Swan E1S0 is already strong enough to tackle almost all types of endgame content available as of 4.1.
