"Acheron..." Merely a borrowed name. You know my nature, remember my deeds... Thus, I am Acheron."
★★★★★

Character Summary

Acheron is a Ultimate-based AoE-oriented DPS who gains her Ultimate through inflicting debuffs onto enemies. She obtains a final DMG multiplier based on the number of Nihility units (other than herself) in her team up to 2, so she calls for at least one Nihility support in her team.

While her damage profile is fairly inflexible, the supports (Nihility or not) that she uses in a fight will greatly affect her performance in specific situations. For optimal performance, you might need 2 different supports (Silver Wolf + Cipher for example) for single-target fights, while you might need 2 other supports (Jiaoqiu + Tribbie) in Pure Fiction-like fights.

Build Notes

Regarding the recommended CR/CD, subtract 12% CR if you are using Izumo and 4% CR/24% CD if you are using Pioneer.

Boots: Always use ATK boots unless you are using Sunday with Acheron, in which case Acheron and Sunday should be 133.34+ SPD with Acheron being faster than Sunday. Aim for 80% CR/200% CD at E0S1 if you are using Sunday.

Orb: ATK orb is better than DMG orb by 1 substat at most. Use whichever orb that has better substats.

◈ Recommended Light Cones / Relic Sets

Light Cones

Along the Passing Shore S1 BiS
Her signature LC and unconditional BiS for her, providing CD and DMG to the majority of her damage. This LC also provides a debuff on attack, which lets Acheron generate 2 Slashed Dream stacks on Skill use instead of 1.
Incessant Rain S1 Alternative
Weaker choice than her signature (the EHR boost from this is wasted), but this LC also provides a debuff on attack, which lets Acheron generate 2 Slashed Dream stacks on Skill use instead of 1. The CR boost is useful and the vulnerability debuff is okay.
Good Night and Sleep Well S5 Alternative
Best gacha 4* choice for her and provides her with a notable DMG boost. Generally a 20-25% drop in performance compared to her signature LC, but this can be lower with more invested supports.
Boundless Choreo S5 Alternative
Good gacha 4* LC if your Acheron has lower relic investment, but this LC has a lower ceiling (4-5% damage loss) than GNSW S5.

Relic Sets

Pioneer DIver of Dead Waters BiS
Her signature set and her unconditional BiS set. Provides her with CR/CD (which she really lacks given that Nihility units do not provide crit) and a small DMG boost.
Poet of Mourning Collapse Alternative
A niche option which provides her with the highest crit increase out of any relic set, but the SPD decrease from this set could result in one fewer action in long enough fights. Theoretically better than Pioneer in shorter fights.
Scholar Lost in Erudition Alternative
Good and efficient set to farm. Provides CR (which she generally lacks) and some DMG for both her Skill and Ultimate, which are her sources of her damage. About a 3-4% decrease from Pioneer.
Crit 2pc / Crit 2pc or ATK 2pc Alternative
For if you don't have any good 4pcs.

Planar Ornaments

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm BiS
Unconditional BiS planar which provides ATK and CR which she particularly lacks with her usual teams.
Inert Salsotto Alternative
Decent alternative planar since this provides CR and DMG to her Ultimate. Usually a 3-4% damage loss from Izumo.

Investment Recommendation

Generally, you should invest in Acheron's supports once you have an E0S1 Acheron. Best supports in order are: SW E2 > Tribbie E1 > Hysilens E0S1 = Cipher E0S1 > Jiaoqiu E0S1. SW E0S1 is on par with Cipher E0S1. If you really want to invest in Acheron, run SW E2 and Tribbie E1. Acheron E2 is a luxury pull but is an okay damage amp for its investment from E0.
